Gina Frankis
Analyst

Gina is responsible for executing transactions, due diligence and financial analysis, and portfolio management. Prior to joining KLH, Gina was an Analyst in the Global Investment Banking division of RBC Capital Markets in New York. Gina graduated from Boston College's Carroll School of Management with a Bachelor of Science in Management and a double concentration in Finance and Management & Leadership, as well as a minor in Ancient Civilization from the Morrissey College of Arts and Sciences.
