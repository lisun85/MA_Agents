James Darnell
Managing Partner

James is responsible for all of the firm’s investment activities and is responsible for the operations of the firm. He is a member of the KLH Investment Committee and serves on the board of directors for a number of KLH portfolio companies. Before joining KLH, he served as CFO and interim CEO at Recovery Holdings, LLC and as a Senior Associate with Founders Investment Banking. James graduated from the University of Alabama with a Bachelor of Science in Finance and a minor in Economics.
