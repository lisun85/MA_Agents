Sean Gilmore
Vice President

Sean is responsible for executing transactions, due diligence, financial analysis, and portfolio management. He serves on the board of directors for a number of KLH portfolio companies and is directly responsible for overseeing Jackson Infrastructure Services, Frankston Packaging, ElectriCom, Apogee Lighting, and BMI Merchandise. He started his career as an Analyst in Atlanta at SunTrust Robinson Humphrey before spending five years with Accel-KKR, a technology-focused private equity firm headquartered in Menlo Park, California. Sean graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Finance and a Master of Science in Finance.
