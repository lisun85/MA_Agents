Ron Moore
Director

Ron is responsible for identifying investment opportunities, including initial financial, operating, and competitive analysis of businesses. Prior to joining KLH, Ron served as an Assistant Vice President at USAmeriBank, where his responsibilities included origination, analysis, and transaction execution on lower middle market senior loans. Ron graduated from Brown University with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and earned a Juris Doctor from Stetson College of Law.
