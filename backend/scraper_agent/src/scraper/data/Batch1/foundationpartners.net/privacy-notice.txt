Foundation Investment Partners (“Foundation Investment Partners, LLC”) operates www.foundationpartners.net and may operate other websites. It is Foundation Investment Partner’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Foundation Investment Partners, LLC collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Foundation Investment Partner’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Foundation Investment Partner’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Foundation Investment Partners, LLC may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

 

Foundation Investment Partners, LLC also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on www.foundationpartners.net blogs/sites. Foundation Investment Partners, LLC only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Foundation Investment Partners, LLC websites choose to interact with Foundation Investment Partners, LLC in ways that require Foundation Investment Partners, LLC to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Foundation Investment Partners, LLC gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at www.foundationpartners.net to provide a username and email address.

Those who engage in transactions with Foundation Investment Partners, LLC are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Foundation Investment Partners, LLC collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Foundation Investment Partners, LLC. Foundation Investment Partners, LLC does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Foundation Investment Partners, LLC may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Foundation Investment Partners, LLC may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Foundation Investment Partners, LLC does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Foundation Investment Partners, LLC uses cookies to help Foundation Investment Partners, LLC identify and track visitors, their usage of Foundation Investment Partners, LLC website, and their website access preferences. Foundation Investment Partners, LLC visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Foundation Investment Partner’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Foundation Investment Partner’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Foundation Investment Partners, LLC and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
