PHIL MCQUITTY
Associate

Phil McQuitty is an Associate and is responsible for evaluating new investment opportunities, executing acquisitions, and supporting Transom Capital’s existing investment portfolio.

Prior to joining Transom Capital, Mr. McQuitty was a Senior Analyst at ACON Investments where he was involved in evaluating transactions in the aerospace & defense, industrials, business services, and consumer sectors. Before that, Phil worked as an Investment Banking Analyst in the Aerospace, Defense, and Government Services Group at Houlihan Lokey in Washington D.C.

Mr. McQuitty holds a B.B.A. in Finance from The George Washington University.
