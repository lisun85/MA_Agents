LEALEI MAGAT-HINO
Vice President, Finance

Lealei Magat-Hino is the Vice President of Finance at Transom Capital Group. In collaboration with the CFO, she manages the firm’s fund administration, reporting, and financial operations. Previously, she served as a Director of Fund Accounting at Trinity Investments for four years, overseeing the accounting operations of the firm’s investment funds. Before her tenure at Trinity, Lealei held positions at Servco Pacific Inc. and First Hawaiian Bank in Honolulu, Hawaii. Prior to relocating to Hawaii, she worked at Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. in Los Angeles. Lealei began her career in the financial services industry at PricewaterhouseCoopers.

Quick Fact

Lealei is an acronym of the first letter of her parents and grandparents’ first names.
