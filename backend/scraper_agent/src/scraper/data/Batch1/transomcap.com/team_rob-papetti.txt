ROB PAPETTI
Vice President

Rob is a Vice President on Transom Capital’s Mergers & Acquisitions team. He is responsible for evaluating and executing investments and provides support on various growth initiatives across the portfolio.

Before joining Transom Capital, Rob was an investment professional at Levine Leichtman Capital Partners in Los Angeles, where he completed investments within the consumer and business services sectors. Earlier in his career, Rob worked in investment banking at J.P. Morgan.

Rob graduated from Indiana University’s Kelley School of Business honors program.

Quick Fact

Rob is a certified deep-sea scuba diver and is likely planning his next trip!
