Operations expertise. Transformative solutions. We dig in to unlock company potential.

With a deep bench of talent, unwavering perseverance, and the ability to troubleshoot all facets of an organization, the Transom Capital team transforms operations and culture to drive value. We focus on middle-market investment opportunities that exhibit great potential which can be unlocked via our operational approach to value creation. Our partnership-driven philosophy generates corporate momentum and productive relationships with both internal and external stakeholders.

Our Approach

Our strategies entail more than board-level strategic interactions. Rooted in operational best practice, Transom’s ARMORSM Value Creation Process and team of functional experts partner with management to design, support and implement solutions tailored to each company’s unique set of opportunities.

Our Team

Our team brings unique urgency and approachability to each situation. We thrive on coming together to solve problems so that, as our name implies, companies emerge stronger—and propelled towards greater outcomes.
