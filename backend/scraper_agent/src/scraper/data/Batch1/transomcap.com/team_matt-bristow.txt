MATT BRISTOW
Senior Vice President, Finance

As Vice President, Finance, Matt partners with the executive leadership teams of Transom’s portfolio companies on finance-related activities such as cash management, forecasting, financial reporting, and compliance. Matt is passionate about supporting the operations of Transom’s portfolio companies and helping the teams identify opportunities for value creation.

Prior to joining Transom, Matt spent approximately ten years in PwC’s Deals practice where he advised both private and public clients through buy-side and sell-side M&A transactions. Matt has led cross-functional teams through a number of complex carve-outs and also has robust experience in the industrial products and consumer markets industries. Before joining the Deals practice, Matt spent several years in PwC’s Assurance practice where he concentrated on the asset management sector.

Matt is a CPA (inactive) and graduated from Missouri State University with a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy and a Master of Science in Accountancy.

Quick Fact

Matt and his wife spent two years on secondment in Amsterdam.
