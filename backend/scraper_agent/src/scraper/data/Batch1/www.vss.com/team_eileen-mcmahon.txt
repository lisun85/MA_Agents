Our Team
Eileen McMahon
Executive Assistant & Project Manager

Eileen McMahon is the Executive Assistant to the Managing Partner and Project Manager at VSS. She joined VSS in 1990. In addition to supporting Jeffrey Stevenson, Eileen manages the administrative and planning functions related to investor relations and special events. Prior to joining VSS, Ms. McMahon worked at Chester Union Free School as a Teacher and Coach.

Eileen holds a BS from SUNY Cortland in Physical Education.
