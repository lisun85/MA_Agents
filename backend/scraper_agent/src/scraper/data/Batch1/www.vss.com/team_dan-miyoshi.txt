Our Team
Dan Miyoshi
Senior Accountant

Dan Miyoshi is a Senior Accountant and joined VSS in 2010.  He is responsible for the valuations of the portfolio companies and day-to-day accounting operations. Prior to joining VSS, he was an analyst at Lehman Brothers in the Private Equity Real Estate division.

Dan holds a MS in accounting from The Peter J. Tobin College of Business at St. John’s University.
