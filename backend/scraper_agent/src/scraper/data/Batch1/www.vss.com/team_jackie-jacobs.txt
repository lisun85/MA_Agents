Jackie Jacobs
Chief Compliance Officer & Director of Operations

Jackie Jacobs is the Chief Compliance Officer & Director of Operations and joined VSS in 2004. She is responsible for the firm’s compliance program, manages day-to-day operations and human resources.

Prior to joining VSS, Jackie held positions with The Boston Consulting Group and Credit Suisse First Boston.

Jackie holds a BS in Economics from Strayer University.
