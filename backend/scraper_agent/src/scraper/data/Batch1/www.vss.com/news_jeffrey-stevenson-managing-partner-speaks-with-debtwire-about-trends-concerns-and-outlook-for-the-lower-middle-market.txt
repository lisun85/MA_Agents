Jeffrey Stevenson, Managing Partner, Speaks With Debtwire About Trends, Concerns And Outlook For The Lower Middle Market.

PODCAST: Jeffrey Stevenson of VSS talks investment strategy, infrastructure, industry multiples and more DECEMBER 8, 2021

‍

On the latest episode of Debtwire North America’s Debtwired!! series, Deputy Editor Reshmi Basu speaks with VSS Managing Partner Jeffrey Stevenson. On this podcast, Jeffrey touches on several topics, including how VSS has changed its strategy during the pandemic. He also delves into the investment climate for lower middle market transactions and chats up growth opportunities in the Architecture Engineer and Construction sector.
