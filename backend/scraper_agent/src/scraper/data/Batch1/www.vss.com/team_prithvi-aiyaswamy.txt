Our Team
Prithvi Aiyaswamy
Analyst

Prithvi Aiyaswamy joined VSS in 2022 and is a member of the Business Development team. He focuses his efforts on sourcing and evaluating new investment opportunities and maintaining intermediary and sponsor relationships.

Prior to joining VSS, Prithvi was on the Strategic Finance and Corporate Development team at Amount, a Chicago-based fintech startup. He was previously a financial contractor working out of the Bay Area, where his placements focused mainly on SaaS businesses.

Prithvi holds a BS in Finance and Entrepreneurship from the Carroll School of Management at Boston College.
