Our Team
Samantha Stevenson
Analyst

Samantha Stevenson is an Analyst and joined VSS in 2023. She is responsible for conducting analysis on potential investments and supporting deal transactions. Additionally, Samantha spearheads initiatives to improve VSS’ ESG program.

Prior to VSS, she held internships at AIG on the Global Capital Markets team, AWE Foundation (a non-profit that provides financial literacy tools for women entrepreneurs), and a microfinance bank in Cameroon.

Samantha holds a BA in International Studies and French from Boston College.
