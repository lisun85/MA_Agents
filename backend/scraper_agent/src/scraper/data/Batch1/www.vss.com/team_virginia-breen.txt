Virginia Breen
Analyst

Virginia Breen joined VSS in 2023 and is a member of the Business Development team.  She is responsible for conducting industry research and external outreach. Prior to joining VSS, Virginia interned at Lincolnshire Management as a member of the deal sourcing and diligence team.

Virginia holds a BBA from the Cox School of Business at Southern Methodist University.
