VSS Promotes Lacey Mehran To Managing Director

NEW YORK – November 11, 2020 – VSS Capital Partners (“VSS”), a private investment firm investing in the healthcare, information, business services and education industries, is pleased to announce the promotion of Lacey Mehran to Managing Director. Mehran is responsible for the firm’s investor relations activities, including fundraising and its co-investment program.

‍

In 2006, Mehran joined VSS as an Associate and worked collaboratively with VSS’ investment team on transaction origination. During her 14-year tenure with the firm, she has been actively involved in several of the firm’s successful acquisitions and has supported and led multiple successful capital-raisings for the firm’s funds and overall been instrumental in growing its investor relationships for the past eight years.

‍

“We are pleased to recognize the hard work and invaluable contributions Lacey has continued to make since joining the firm,” said Jeffrey Stevenson, Managing Partner of VSS. “Her years of dedication in building and maintaining our relationships with investors, both in the U.S. and globally, combined with her investment acumen and focus on teamwork have helped ensure VSS is well positioned for further growth for years to come.”

‍

Prior to joining VSS, Ms. Mehran worked at a leading equity research firm, OTA-OTR. Ms. Mehran received an M.B.A. from The Yale School of Management and a B.S. in Applied Mathematics from North Carolina State University.

‍

About VSS
VSS is a private investment firm that invests in the healthcare, information, business services and education industries. Headquartered in New York, VSS provides capital for growth financing, recapitalizations, strategic acquisitions, and buyouts to lower middle market companies and management teams with the goal of building companies organically as well as through a focused add-on acquisition program. VSS makes privately-negotiated investments across the capital structure and invests in situations requiring control or non-control equity, mezzanine securities, and structured equity securities. VSS has over three decades of experience, made investments in 88 portfolio companies, with over 350 add-on acquisitions, and manages $3.5 billion in aggregate committed capital across 7 funds. For more information, please visit: www.vss.com.
