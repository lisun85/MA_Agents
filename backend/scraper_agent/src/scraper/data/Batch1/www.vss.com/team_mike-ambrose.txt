Our Team
Mike Ambrose
Controller

Mike Ambrose is the Controller and joined VSS in 1997. His responsibilities include financial reporting for VSS SC III as well financial reporting, budgeting, cash management and managing accounts payable, accounts receivable and payroll for the management company.  

Prior to joining VSS, Mike was the Controller for Wine Enthusiast Companies, a magazine publisher and online retailer.

Mike holds an MBA from Long Island University and a BS in Accounting and Information Systems from Queens College of the City of New York.
