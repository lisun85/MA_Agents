Rhodes Garner
Senior Associate

Rhodes Garner is a Senior Associate and joined the firm in 2022. He is responsible for evaluating, executing, and monitoring investments. Prior to joining VSS, Rhodes was an Investment Banking analyst in the Financial Institutions Group at UBS, where he focused on M&A, Equity and Debt Transactions across the FinTech, Insurance and Asset Management verticals.

Rhodes holds a BS in Finance from Georgetown University.
