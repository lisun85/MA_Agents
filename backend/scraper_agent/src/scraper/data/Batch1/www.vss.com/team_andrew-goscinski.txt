Our Team
Andrew Goscinski
Partner, Chief Financial Officer, and Senior Managing Director

Andrew Goscinski is a Partner, Chief Financial Officer, and Senior Managing Director. He joined VSS in 1998. Andrew is responsible for the Firm’s investment activities and participates in the accounting, structuring, tax planning and administration of the funds. Andrew oversees all the financial functions of the Firm, including the accounting and controls, budgeting, risk management and tax planning. He prepares and reviews all the Firm’s financials related to the funds and its limited partners.

Prior to joining VSS, Andrew was a fund accountant at Citibank Global Asset Management. He began his career as a staff accountant at Sanwa Securities.

Andrew holds a BS in Accounting with Honors from The College of New Jersey.
