Our Team
Jordan Margolin
Head of Business Development

Jordan Margolin joined VSS in 2021 and leads the firm’s business development efforts. He is responsible for identifying and evaluating investment opportunities and focuses his efforts on deal origination, intermediary and sponsor coverage. Prior to joining VSS, Jordan worked in investment banking in the financial services group at Piper Sandler, where he focused on M&A and capital raises for banks and thrifts across the United States.

Jordan holds a MS in Accounting and BS in Finance and International Development from Tulane University.
