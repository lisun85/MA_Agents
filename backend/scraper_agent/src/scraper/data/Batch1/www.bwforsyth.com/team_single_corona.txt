CAM CORONA
Senior Associate

Cam joined the team in 2021 as an Associate, with responsibility for sourcing, evaluating, and executing M&A transactions and supporting our operating companies’ senior leadership teams on key strategic initiatives.

Previously, Cam spent two years as an Analyst at Lincoln International, a global middle market investment bank. While at Lincoln, Cam provided executional support on a range of M&A transactions in the industrials group.

Outside the office, Cam values quality time with his wife, Miranda, and their children, Louis and Clara Brigid. In his free time, he enjoys cycling the streets of St. Louis, unwinding with a good book, exploring local hiking trails, and firing up the grill for a barbecue with family and friends.
