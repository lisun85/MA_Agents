WALKERHUGHES
Indianapolis, IN
Investment: September 2023

WalkerHughes is a privately held, founder-led retail insurance brokerage business headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana. WalkerHughes provides commercial lines, personal lines, and employee benefits services, with integrated operations and a leading proprietary technology system. WalkerHughes has grown organically and through 25+ acquisitions of culturally aligned retail agencies in the Midwest.
