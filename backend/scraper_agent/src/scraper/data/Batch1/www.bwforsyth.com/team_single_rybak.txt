ARTHUR RYBAK
Vice President

Arthur joined the team in 2017 as an Associate, with responsibility for sourcing, evaluating, and executing M&A transactions, while supporting our operating companies’ senior leadership teams on strategy development and key organic growth initiatives.


Previously, Arthur spent nearly three years as an Analyst at Robert W. Baird, a middle market investment bank. While at Robert W. Baird, Arthur provided executional support on a variety of M&A and financing transactions in the energy group.


Outside of the office, Arthur enjoys spending time with his family and friends, traveling and exploring different cities, attending concerts and sporting events, and trying new local restaurants.
