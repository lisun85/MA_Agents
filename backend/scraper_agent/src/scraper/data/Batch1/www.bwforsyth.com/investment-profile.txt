INVESTMENT PROFILE
BUILDING GREAT BUSINESSES THROUGH PEOPLE

BW Forsyth Partners (Forsyth) is a St. Louis-based investment firm with a perpetual investment horizon and permanent capital base. Forsyth focuses on acquiring and building enduring businesses through a people-centric approach to culture, programmatic acquisitions and operational strategy deployment.
