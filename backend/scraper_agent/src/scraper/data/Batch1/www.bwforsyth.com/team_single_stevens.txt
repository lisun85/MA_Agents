JOE STEVENS
Vice President of Business Development

Joe joined the team in 2022 as the Vice President of Business Development, with responsibility for sourcing new platforms, add-on businesses, and driving the overall business development and marketing function within BW Forsyth Partners.

Previously, Joe spent over 5 years within the Financial & Risk division of the London Stock Exchange Group (LSEG). While at LSEG, Joe managed strategic client relationships, drove new business, and acted as a data and workflow expert for the 3rd Party Risk business.

Outside of the office, Joe enjoys spending time with his wife, Kaitlin, and son, Lane, playing local golf courses with friends, and being outdoors.
