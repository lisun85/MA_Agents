MACHINE SOLUTIONS, INC.
Flagstaff, AZ
Acquired: November 2011

Machine Solutions Inc. is a leading provider of process automation and testing equipment to the medical device industry. Based in Flagstaff, AZ, Machine Solutions provides proprietary mechanical solutions to a variety of complex process, testing and device design challenges and has become the industry standard for stent crimping, balloon catheter pleating & folding, marker band swaging, and many other types of equipment. MSI has been instrumental in automating manual processes within catheter and stent manufacturing operations and the medical device industry.

For more information, visit www.machinesolutions.com.
