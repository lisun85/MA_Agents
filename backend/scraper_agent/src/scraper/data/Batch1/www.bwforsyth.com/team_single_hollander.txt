BRENDAN HOLLANDER
Senior Associate

Brendan joined the team in 2020 as an Associate, with responsibility for sourcing, evaluating, and executing M&A transactions and supporting our operating companies’ senior leadership teams on key strategic initiatives.

Previously, Brendan spent two years as an Analyst at Robert W. Baird, a global middle market investment bank. While at Baird, Brendan provided executional support on a variety of M&A and financing transactions in the energy group.

Outside of the office, Brendan loves spending time with his wife, Jess, and son, Theo. In addition, Brendan enjoys traveling to new golf courses and rooting on his St. Louis teams, Kansas Jayhawks, and Pittsburgh Steelers.
