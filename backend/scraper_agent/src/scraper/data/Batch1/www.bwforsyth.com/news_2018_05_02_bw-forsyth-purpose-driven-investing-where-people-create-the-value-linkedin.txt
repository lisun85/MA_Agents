BW FORSYTH: PURPOSE-DRIVEN INVESTING, WHERE PEOPLE CREATE THE VALUE (LINKEDIN)
LinkedIn Article by Matt Sonefeldt

People. Purpose. Performance. Those are the drivers behind the success of purpose-driven company BW Forsyth Partners.

BW Forsyth specializes in acquiring midsize companies with a path to a brighter future, but who may not possess the tools or resources to get there. The BW Forsyth team then helps these firms meet their untapped potential, particularly when it comes to people. We recently spoke with co-founders Kyle Chapman and Ryan Gable to learn more about their ‘people first’ philosophy of investing and running companies.
