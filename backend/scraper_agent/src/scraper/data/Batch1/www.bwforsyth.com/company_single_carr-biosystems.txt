CARR BIOSYSTEMS
Clearwater, FL
Acquired: December 2023

CARR Biosystems is a life science partner accelerating bioseparations to support the development and commercialization of advanced therapies. CARR Biosystems offers an innovative range of separation equipment including single-use and stainless-steel centrifuges tailored to enhance the production of biological products and support seamless scaling of customer processes from pre-clinical to commercial stages.

To learn more, visit www.carrbiosystems.com.
