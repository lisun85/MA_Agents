FIRE Capital Fund has consistently focused on building stakeholder value through ‘an activist model’ where it adds value across the real estate development chain.
Investment Philosophy

The FIRE Capital Fund takes strategic equity positions in real estate development projects and plays the role of an activist private equity fund with active participation in each investee company across the development value chain ?from assessing the market for the right product offer to implementation strategies for an international quality product, from program management to formulating effective marketing strategies. FIRE Capital Fund targets high quality real estate investments with strong fundamentals and high growth potential.

Sector & Location Focus

FIRE Capital Fund targets investments in residential, multi-use developments centered around residential use, office complexes, software technology parks, retail malls and leisure & entertainment developments. FIRE Capital Fund is particularly interested in suburban metro locations, Tier II and Tier III cities.
