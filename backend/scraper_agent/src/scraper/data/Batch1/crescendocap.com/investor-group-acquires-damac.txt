MAY 28, 2013
Investor Group Acquires Damac.
Damac Products, a custom manufacturer of infrastructure solutions for the data center market, has been acquired by a group that includes Prairie Capital, Crescendo Capital Partners and Pleasant Bay Capital Partners. Based in La Mirada, CA, Damac makes server racks, wall mount cabinets, aisle containment solutions, cable runways and power distribution units.
