Mike’s primary responsibilities as president include enhancing the firm’s capabilities to improve focus areas, leading the Operations Team, collaborating with deal selection on transaction due diligence, and portfolio company management including Exits. His greatest satisfaction comes from partnering with great management teams to support them as they grow their business.

Prior to joining HKW, Mike worked for Sensata Technologies and held several financial and operational management positions at Control Devices, Inc., a former HKW portfolio company.

Mike lives in New Hampshire with his wife and their three sons. Outside the office, their family enjoys being outdoors and all the activities the Northeast has to offer.
