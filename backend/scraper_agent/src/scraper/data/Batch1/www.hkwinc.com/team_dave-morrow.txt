Dave’s primary focus and expertise is in operational process improvements, process mapping and optimization, implementing lean manufacturing and Six Sigma principles. He collaborates with executive leadership to develop business process improvements.
 
Prior to this role Dave was COO of ProAct Services, a previous HKW investment. He also has held various operational leadership roles within the automotive, commercial products, and aerospace sectors.
 
Dave lives in beautiful Ludington, MI on the shores of Lake Michigan. When not helping portfolio companies, he enjoys spending time with his family, motorcycling, off-roading and most things outdoor related.   

*HKW’s Operating Executives, while not employees, members, personnel, or affiliates of HKW, are leaders in their respective fields who are available to provide industry guidance to HKW and may make significant contributions to HKW, its portfolio companies, and investors.
