JENNIFER SWINEA
Position: Executive Assistant/Office ManagerCategories: team

Ms. Swinea is the Executive Assistant & Office Manager of Branford Castle. She oversees the day-to-day office operations, coordinates all corporate events, and assists with other key company activities. Before joining Branford Castle in 2023, Ms. Swinea worked as an EA at Brown Advisory, a Baltimore-based wealth management firm; she was also an equities trader with Schonfeld Securities. Ms. Swinea graduated Cum Laude from NC State University with a BS in Finance, Economics minor.
