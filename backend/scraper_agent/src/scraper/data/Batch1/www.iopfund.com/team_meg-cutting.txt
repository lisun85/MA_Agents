MEG CUTTING

Administrative Assistant

> WITH IOP SINCE 2023

Meg is the Administrative Assistant at IOP. She works alongside the Office Manager to support day-to-day organizational and administrative needs of the office staff and executives.

Meg is also a classical organist and keyboard accompanist. Prior to joining IOP, she worked in the classical music field and concertized as a soloist and accompanist. Meg holds a Bachelor of Music from the Eastman School of Music and a Master of Music from Yale University.
