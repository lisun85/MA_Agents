NICK DIMAURO

Associate Vice President, Operations

> WITH IOP SINCE 2023

Nick has several years of experience in the areas of financial planning and analysis, process improvement, profitability modeling, and pricing and mix management. At IOP, he works with the management teams of IOP’s portfolio companies and with the firm’s Operating Principals to implement key operational and financial initiatives.

Prior to joining IOP, Nick led the FP&A function at Hometown Food Company, a Brynwood Partners portfolio company. Previously, he worked at The Kraft Heinz Company, where he worked in a variety of finance roles. Nick began his career within Deloitte’s Financial Advisory practice.

Nick holds a B.S. in Industrial Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and an MBA with honors from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business.
