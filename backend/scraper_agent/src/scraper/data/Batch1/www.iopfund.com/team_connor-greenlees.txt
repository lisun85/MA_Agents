CONNOR GREENLEES

Vice President

> WITH IOP SINCE 2016

Connor is a Vice President at IOP, responsible for deal sourcing and execution as well as managing portfolio investments.

Prior to joining IOP, Connor served as an Analyst at Houlihan Lokey, Inc., focusing on middle-market mergers and acquisitions. Previously, he worked as an investment banker at Brown Gibbons Lang & Company.

Connor earned a B.S. in engineering from Miami University (Oxford, Ohio) and a Master of Engineering and Management from Case Western Reserve University.
