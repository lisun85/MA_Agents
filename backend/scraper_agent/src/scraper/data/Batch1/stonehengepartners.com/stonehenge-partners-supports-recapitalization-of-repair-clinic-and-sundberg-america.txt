Stonehenge Partners Supports Recapitalization of Repair Clinic and Sundberg America

December 2016:  Stonehenge Partners supported the recapitalization of Repair Clinic, LLC (“Repair Clinic”) and Sundberg America, LLC (“Sundberg America”). 

Headquartered in Canton, MI,  Repair Clinic is a distributor of replacement and maintenance parts for appliances, lawn equipment, HVAC equipment, and power tools for do-it-yourself (“DIY”) consumers.

Headquartered in Chicago, IL,  Sundberg America is a national wholesale distributor of replacement parts for appliances and HVAC systems to dealers and technicians as well as national accounts.

Repair Clinic and Sundberg America are owned by Burke America, a Chicago-based private holding company.
