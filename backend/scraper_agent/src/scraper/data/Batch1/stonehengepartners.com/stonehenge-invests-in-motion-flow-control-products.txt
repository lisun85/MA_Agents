Stonehenge Invests in Motion & Flow Control Products

December 2024: In partnership with Colville Group (“Colville”), Stonehenge Partners (“Stonehenge”) has completed a growth investment in Motion & Flow Control Products (“MFCP” or the “Company”).

About MFCP

Headquartered in Denver, Colorado, MFCP is a leading distributor of fluid connector and fluid power solutions, including hydraulic, automation, instrumentation, filtration, seal and valve products. The Company was created in 2011 by the merger of McCoy Sales Corporation and Fluid Connector Products, Inc., and has been backed by Colville since 2006. MFCP serves industrial customers through more than 50 locations across the Western United States. MFCP is aligned with industry leading manufacturing partners and is Parker Hannifin’s largest distributor, with ParkerStore locations supplying OEM and MRO customers with direct access to engineered industrial products and factory-trained personnel.

About Stonehenge Partners

Stonehenge is an Ohio-based private equity firm that focuses exclusively on investing in leading middle market companies. Founded in 1999, Stonehenge has a long history of partnering with entrepreneurs and managers who are focused on building winning teams, accelerating growth, and creating shareholder value. Stonehenge provides flexible capital that meets the needs of all stakeholders, including majority equity, minority equity, debt, and combinations thereof. Stonehenge has raised more than $1.4 billion of committed capital and is currently investing out of its sixth institutional fund.

About Colville Group

Based in Charlotte, NC, Colville Group is a private investment partnership dedicated to building private companies in the lower middle market. For over 20 years, Colville has partnered with management teams to accelerate growth of owner-operated industrial companies.
