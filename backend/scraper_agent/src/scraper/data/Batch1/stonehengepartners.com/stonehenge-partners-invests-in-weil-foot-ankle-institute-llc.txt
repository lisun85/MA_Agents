Stonehenge Partners Invests in Weil Foot & Ankle Institute, LLC

July 2019:  Stonehenge Partners has invested in Weil Foot & Ankle Institute, LLC (“WFAI” or the “Company”). Headquartered in Des Plaines, Illinois, WFAI provides a full range of podiatry services and surgeries throughout Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin in inpatient and outpatient settings. 

Founded in 1965, the Company has established itself as the leading podiatric medicine practitioner serving the Chicagoland area with approximately 30 physicians. For additional information, please visit WFAI’s website.
