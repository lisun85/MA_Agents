Stonehenge Partners Acquires Queen City Hospice, LLC

March 2018: Stonehenge Partners has acquired Queen City Hospice, LLC (“QCH” or the “Company”) to support the management team in the continued growth of the business. Headquartered in Columbus, Ohio, QCH is a provider of hospice services for patients with terminal diagnoses. This business will serve as Stonehenge’s platform investment in the hospice industry, with the expectation to grow organically and through acquisition.

The Company specializes in providing physical, emotional, and spiritual care for individuals with an advanced illness as well as emotional and psychosocial support for their families. The Company is dedicated to providing patient-centered care and support to residents of the Midwestern United States. Whether patients live in a private residence, an assisted living community, or a nursing home, the Company’s well-trained and experienced team members will go to patients’ locations to provide personalized assistance and care.
