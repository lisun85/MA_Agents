Stonehenge Partners Supports Acquisition of Gamma Aerospace, LLC

February 2017:  Stonehenge Partners supported the acquisition of Gamma Aerospace, LLC (“Gamma” or the “Company”). Located in Mansfield, Texas, Gamma is a manufacturer of engineered airframe and flight components for leading original equipment manufacturers (“OEMs”) and Tier I suppliers in the aerospace and defense (“A&D”) industry. 

The Company manufactures a diverse offering of components for the A&D industry through machining, stamping, heat-treating, stretch-forming, and titanium hot-forming processes. Gamma specializes in complex, multi-contoured parts and multi-axis machined components for both interior and exterior applications on an aircraft. Focusing on both high-volume and low-volume batches, the Company delivers over 125,000 parts annually to a blue-chip customer base of industry-leading manufacturers and Tier I suppliers.
