Frontpoint Health ("Frontpoint") is a leading healthcare services company focused on providing the continuum of care in the home to the senior population. Through close integration with its health system and health plan partners, Frontpoint delivers superior patient health outcomes while minimizing total spend to the healthcare system. Frontpoint is one of largest and fastest-growing home health and hospice companies in the United States.
