Dane Block Jr. is an Associate at LCM and is responsible for performing due diligence on, executing, and managing investments across healthcare industry sectors.

Mr. Block started his career at Stephens Inc. as an Investment Banking Analyst before becoming an Associate in 2021. While at Stephens, Mr. Block focused on executing mergers and acquisitions for clients across various healthcare verticals.

Mr. Block graduated Cum Laude from Auburn University with a BBA in Finance.
