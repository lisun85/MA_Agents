Latticework Capital Management Expands Its Team
DALLAS, TEXAS
MARCH 27, 2017

Latticework Capital Management (“Latticework”) is pleased to announce the expansion of its team with the hiring of Alek Blankenau.

Mr. Blankenau joins the Latticework team as Vice President, responsible for sourcing, executing, and managing investments across healthcare industry sectors. Previously, he was employed at Lone Star Investment Advisors, a middle-market private equity group where he was responsible for due diligence, transaction execution, portfolio company financing, and portfolio monitoring. Prior to Lone Star, Mr. Blankenau worked for Cogent Partners, an investment bank focused on secondary market transactions. With this hire, the team at Latticework now totals four individuals, including founders Steve Neumann and Kyle Bradford.

“We are excited that Alek has joined the team and look forward to working together to grow our firm into the future,” said Managing Partner Kyle Bradford.

About Latticework Capital Management

Founded in 2014, Latticework Capital Management (“Latticework”) is a Dallas, Texas-based private equity group focused on making control equity investments in lower-middle market healthcare companies across the United States. Latticework leverages its over 40 combined years of healthcare experience, as well as its network of industry executives, to help companies grow and realize their full long-term value.
