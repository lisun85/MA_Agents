Mr. Fletcher is a Managing Director of LCM and is responsible for sourcing, executing and managing investments across healthcare industry sectors. He is currently a member of the Board of Directors of Beacon Behavioral Holdings, inReach Health, and Kalon Aesthetics. He formerly served as a Board Observer of American Veterinary Group.

Mr. Fletcher was previously a Senior Associate at American Capital, Ltd. where he focused on executing debt and equity investments across the life sciences, healthcare services and business services industries, among others. During his tenure at American Capital, Mr. Fletcher served as a Board Observer of multiple American Capital portfolio companies, including The Meadows, Scientific Protein Laboratories, TestAmerica and Tensar Corporation. Prior to joining American Capital, Mr. Fletcher worked in the investment banking division of Stephens Inc. where he focused on merger and acquisition, capital markets and financing transactions.

Mr. Fletcher graduated cum laude with a BBA in Finance and magna cum laude with a BA in Spanish from Southern Methodist University.
