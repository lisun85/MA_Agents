Will Fletcher Promoted to Managing Director
JANUARY 29, 2025

We are excited to announce the promotion of Will Fletcher to the position of Managing Director at Latticework Capital Management.

This well-deserved promotion is a testament to Will’s exceptional leadership, dedication, and hard work. Will has been with Latticework since its founding in 2015 and has consistently demonstrated a commitment to excellence and has played a pivotal role in driving our firm’s success. We are confident that in this new role, he will continue to inspire and lead our team to even greater heights.

Will Fletcher

Managing Director
