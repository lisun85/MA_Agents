Nina Saboorian is a Senior Associate of LCM and is responsible for executing and managing investments across various healthcare industry sectors. She is currently a Board Observer of Healthcare Building Solutions and Athenix Body Sculpting Institute.

Ms. Saboorian was previously an Investment Banking Analyst with Houlihan Lokey. While at Houlihan Lokey, Ms. Saboorian focused on executing mergers and acquisitions for clients across various healthcare verticals.

Ms. Saboorian graduated from Southern Methodist University with a BBA in Finance.
