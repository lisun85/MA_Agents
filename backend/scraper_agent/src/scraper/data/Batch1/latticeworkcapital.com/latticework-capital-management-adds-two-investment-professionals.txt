Latticework Capital Management Adds Two Investment Professionals
JUNE 8, 2018

Latticework Capital Management, LLC, a healthcare private equity firm based in Dallas, TX announced that it has hired TJ Shepherd and Travis Hennessey as Senior Associate and Associate, respectively.

Prior to joining LCM, Mr. Shepherd was a Senior Associate at Lone Star Funds, a Texas based multi-strategy private equity firm. While at Lone Star Funds, Mr. Shepherd worked on the origination, underwriting, and asset management of corporate investments in private equity and private credit. Prior to Lone Star Funds, Mr. Shepherd worked at Crestline Investors, where he focused on the underwriting and asset management of credit related hedge fund investments. He holds a BBA with concentrations in Finance, Marketing and Accounting from Texas Christian University.

Mr. Hennessey was previously an Investment Banking Analyst with Houlihan Lokey where he focused on executing mergers and acquisitions for clients across various healthcare verticals. Mr. Hennessey holds a BBA in Accounting from Texas Christian University.

About LCM

Headquartered in Dallas, TX, with an office in Denver, CO, Latticework is a growth oriented private equity firm focused exclusively on making control equity investments in the healthcare industry. LCM has completed more than 130 transactions across a diverse array of healthcare subsectors and currently has more than $550 million in assets under management. LCM leverages its more than 100 combined years of healthcare and investing experience, as well as its network of industry executives, to help companies grow and realize their full long-term value. For more information, please visit www.latticeworkcapital.com.
