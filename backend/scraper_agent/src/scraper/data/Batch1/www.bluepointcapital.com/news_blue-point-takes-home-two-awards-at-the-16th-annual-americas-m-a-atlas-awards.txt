Blue Point is honored to have been named USA Private Equity Firm of the Year and also to be awarded Private Equity Deal of the Year for the acquisition of National Safety Apparel at the 16th Annual Americas M&A Atlas Awards by Global M&A Network. These awards recognize the achievements of successful dealmakers, outstanding firms and the best value-creating transactions.

In addition to this year’s recognitions, Blue Point has been acknowledged by the Global M&A Network organization for past achievements, including:

2024 USA Private Equity Add-On Deal of the Year for Transtar Holding Company’s Acquisition of Arch Auto Parts
2024 Top USA Woman Dealmaker – Megan Kneipp
2023 USA Private Equity Deal of the Year for Acquisition of Kendall Vegetation Services

Congratulations to the NSA deal team and to all NSA employees who have helped the company become the leader it is today. Blue Point is excited to be part of the next chapter!
