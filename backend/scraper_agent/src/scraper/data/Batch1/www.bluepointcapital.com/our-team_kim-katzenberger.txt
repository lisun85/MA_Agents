Kim brings a wealth of experience in leading lower middle-market companies across diverse sectors including consumer goods, industrial products, technology and business services — driving growth, delivering strategic insights and transforming finance & accounting functions. In his role, Kim will be instrumental in supporting our team with evaluating investment opportunities, planning and executing integrations and aligning operating finance strategies to optimize business performance. Most recently, Kim served as Group CFO for Riverside’s Microcap Fund, where he worked closely with portfolio companies to drive meaningful improvements in their finance organizations. Prior to that, he was CFO of Fiji Water, an international consumer products company. Earlier in his career, Kim held key leadership roles at General Electric, including Vice President of FP&A for NBC’s TV Group, Finance Integration Leader at GE Consumer Products, CFO of GE’s LED Lighting division, and Director of FP&A at MSNBC Cable.

Kim earned his A.B. in Economics from Dartmouth and an M.B.A. from New York University’s Stern School of Business.
