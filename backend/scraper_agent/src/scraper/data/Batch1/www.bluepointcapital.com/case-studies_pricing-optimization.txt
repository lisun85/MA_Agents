Pricing Analytically And Strategically
We’re focused on helping our teams generate incremental margin by managing pricing more analytically, strategically and systematically.
It’s been particularly applicable to businesses with high customer counts, high SKU counts and high order frequencies.
Steps to Gains
Building the requisite technology stack.
Rigorously measuring fully burdened customer and product profitability.
Creating granular customer, product and channel segments by size and profitability.
Prioritizing areas for price increases with greatest potential impact and least risk.
Operationalizing and maintaining gains via process, tightened controls and exception monitoring.
