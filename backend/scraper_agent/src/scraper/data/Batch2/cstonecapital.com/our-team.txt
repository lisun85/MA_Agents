Our Team

The Cornerstone team has deep experience arranging, structuring and executing acquisitions in a variety of industries as well as managing investments post-closing.

Jonathan H. Alt
Principal

Download vcard

Mr. Alt is a Founder of Cornerstone. For the two years prior to forming Cornerstone, he was involved in industry consolidations, mergers, and acquisitions with BGL Capital Partners, a Chicago-based merchant banking group. Prior to that, he was an Associate with the merchant banking group of Banque Paribas, where he was involved in arranging, structuring, and financing leveraged buyout transactions in a variety of industries. Mr. Alt received a B.A. in Finance from the College of Commerce and Business Administration at the University of Illinois.

Lili Zhou
Chief Financial Officer

Download vcard

Ms. Zhou joined Cornerstone in 2013 as CFO after leading financial activities at RSA Engineered Products, where she also served as CFO, and Klune Industries/PCC where she worked as a Corporate Finance executive for over eleven years. Tim Martin
Executive Advisor/ Board Member

 

Tim is presently an Executive Advisor and Board Member for Cornerstone. He recently retired from Doncasters in 2018 after five years of service as Chief Commercial Officer where he had group responsibility for Sourcing, Strategic Business Development, Sales and M&A focusing on the Aerospace and IGT markets. Tim was also President of Doncasters Inc., USA from 2012 through 2018. Prior to that, he spent 35 years at UTC Aerospace Systems (formerly Goodrich) before retiring as Vice President, Supply Chain and Strategic Initiatives. While there, he also served as Chairman of the Board for GATT – Tianjin China. Tim has a Bachelor degree in Business Administration and is also a certified “Lean Manufacturing” Practitioner with extensive continuous improvement experience. He currently volunteers at Interfaith Services in Carlsbad, CA. as career counselor for those in need.

Zach Pavlosky
Director – Business Development

 

Zach joined Cornerstone Capital in 2025 to lead the firm’s business development efforts.  Prior to joining Cornerstone, he spent over a decade in investment banking, advising companies on M&A and capital raising where he primarily focused on business development and building relationships with executives and business owners needing transaction advisory services.  He has advised on over $300 million in transaction value, working with companies in a number of industries including industrials, healthcare, business services and technology.  Zach received a B.S. in Pre-Medicine from Virginia Tech and has been a CFA charter holder since 2018.
