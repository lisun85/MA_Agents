About Us

Formed in 2000 and with offices in Fort Worth and Philadelphia, Cornerstone Capital Holdings has grown both organically and through acquisition to become a leader in aerospace, defense, space and industrial manufacturing.

Our success begins with the talented management teams who lead each portfolio business with a relentless commitment to serve our customer, innovate within our markets, and drive efficiency and growth throughout the organization.

We continue to strive for organic growth as well as opportunities to acquire new, middle-market businesses that will fit within our organization.

Investment Criteria

Cornerstone seeks to acquire low-tech manufacturing and industrial service businesses with enterprise values up to $100 million.
