JOHN OOMMEN
SENIOR ASSOCIATE

John has seven years of private equity advisory experience. 

John focuses on investment analysis, financial modeling, due diligence and other activities in support of the firm’s investing activities, while assisting with portfolio company analyses and projects.

Prior to John’s time with Rock Gate, he provided sell side equity research covering a universe of cement, metals, power, capital goods, banking and NBFC companies.

He received his MBA from Neville Wadia Institute Of Management Studies and Research and Undergrad in Accounting and Business Management.

John is a CFA Charterholder.
