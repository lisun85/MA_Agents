Eliza Reinisch
ASSOCIATE

Ms. Reinisch joined Granite Creek as an Associate in 2024. As a member of the investment team, she is responsible for deal screening, transaction execution, and investment portfolio management. Prior to Granite Creek, she was a Consultant at EY-Parthenon, where she worked on a variety of buy-side transactions and supported businesses with corporate and growth strategy. Ms. Reinisch holds a B.S. in Finance and Management from the Wharton School.
