The Chia Co
NEW YORK, NY

The Chia Company is one of the largest marketers and distributors of natural, raw Chia seeds worldwide. The Chia Company, through exclusive grower relationships and years of agronomical work, supplies global food manufacturers, as well as produces its own brand of chia products stocked in national retailers such as Whole Foods Markets. Founded in Australia in 2003, The Chia Company is based in New York.
