Life Spine, Inc.
HUNTLEY, IL

Life Spine, Inc. is a medical device company that makes proprietary spinal implants used to treat a wide range of spinal injuries and deformities. The company’s products are FDA-approved and supported by clinical data and a robust, global intellectual property portfolio of 180+ patents and 220+ filed patent applications. Life Spine is one of the leading players in minimally-invasive procedures; their proprietary micro-invasive™ expandable implants reduce operating time, cost, and post-op complications. The Company is headquartered in Huntley, IL.
