Pete Pacelli
PRINCIPAL

Mr. Pacelli joined Granite Creek as a Principal in 2024, bringing with him over 12 years of finance, investment, and operations experience. Prior to joining GC, Mr. Pacelli was a Principal at Knox Capital Partners for over six years, where he was responsible for sourcing, evaluating, and executing new investment opportunities, as well as working with portfolio companies. Mr. Pacelli began his career at Bank of America before joining Wind Point Partners, a Chicago-based middle-market private equity firm. He also founded Victory Views, a managed services business serving the education industry. Mr. Pacelli is a veteran of the U.S. Navy, where he served as an intelligence officer.
