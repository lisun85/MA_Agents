Granite Creek Capital Partners Named to Inc.’s 2020 Private Equity 50 List
OCTOBER 27, 2020

CHICAGO, Oct. 27, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — Granite Creek Capital Partners, L.L.C., a private investment firm based in Chicago, today announced that it was named to Inc.’s 2020 Private Equity 50 list. The second annual Inc. top 50 Founder-Friendly Private Equity Firms list honors the most founder-friendly PE firms that have a successful track record of supporting and partnering with entrepreneurs. To see the complete list, please visit: http://inc.com/private-equity.
