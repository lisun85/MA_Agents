Auto Rescue Solutions
CARROLLTON, TX

Auto Rescue Solutions is a dispatcher of roadside assistance providers who support motorists on behalf of Auto Rescue’s customers (motor clubs / insurers). With resources spanning metropolitan regions across the country, Auto Rescue is the roadside assistance company upon which motor clubs and insurers rely to get motorists back on the road. Auto Rescue Solutions is headquartered in Carrollton, TX.
