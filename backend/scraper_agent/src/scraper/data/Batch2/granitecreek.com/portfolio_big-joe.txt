Big Joe
LOMBARD, IL

Big Lift d/b/a Big Joe, is a leading manufacturer and distributor of Class III forklift trucks, also known as “electric walkie” forklift trucks. In addition, the Company also provides parts, refurbishing, and repair services to its customers. The Company sells through a dealer network and services national retail customers directly. Many of these accounts have been customers for over 15 years. The Big Joe products are well known among the world’s top forklift dealers.
