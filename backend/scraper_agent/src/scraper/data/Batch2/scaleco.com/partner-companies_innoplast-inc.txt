Overview

Founded in 2005 and based in Garfield Heights, OH, Innoplast, Inc. (“Innoplast” or the “Company”) is a manufacturer and distributor of vehicle, facility, and pedestrian safety products.

The Company’s product catalog, which includes bollard covers, clearance bars, and other conspicuity solutions designed to make commercial spaces safer, are used nationwide across a variety of end-markets, such as restaurants, car washes, fire stations, healthcare facilities, retail, warehouses, airports, and parking garages, among others.
