Meet ScaleCo’s Director of Finance* - Dan Pioch
Dan Pioch

Director of Finance*

Dan Pioch joined the ScaleCo Capital team in 2024 as Director of Finance and part of ScaleCo’s Scale Creation Team. In his role, he oversees portfolio company financial controls, monthly reporting, quarterly forecasting, annual budgeting, and audits.

Prior to joining ScaleCo, Dan held multiple positions at both The Siegfried Group and PwC where he focused on advising public and private clients on both the buy-side and sell-side as well as helping with financial due diligence, audits, and corporate finance activities.

Dan is originally from Concord, OH. He is a CPA and earned dual degrees in Accounting and Finance from Miami University’s Farmer School of Business.

*Dan is an independent contractor; not an employee of ScaleCo Capital.
