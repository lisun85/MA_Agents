Director of Fund Marketing & Operations

Holly Smith joined ScaleCo Capital in 2021 and currently serves as Director of Fund Marketing & Operations. Holly is originally from Scotch Plains, NJ. She earned a BS in Marketing from the University of Delaware where she was a member of the diving team.
