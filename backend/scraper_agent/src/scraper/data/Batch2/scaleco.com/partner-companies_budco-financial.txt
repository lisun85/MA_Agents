Overview

Founded in 2002 and headquartered in Detroit, MI, Budco Financial (“Budco” or the “Company”) is a payment plan program management platform that specializes in serving automotive dealerships nationwide.

Budco offers comprehensive, interest-free installment payment plans designed to help agents, dealers, and administrators sell more vehicle service contracts and drive incremental revenue streams for the dealerships.

With 7,000+ enrolled dealerships across the U.S. and Canada and long-term relationships with many of the global leaders in automotive manufacturing, Budco helps dealerships offer their customers the ability to pay for an extended service contract over time, on their terms.
