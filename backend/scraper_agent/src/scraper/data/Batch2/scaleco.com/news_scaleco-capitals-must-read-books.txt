ScaleCo Capital’s Must-Read Books

At ScaleCo Capital, we believe that continuous learning is essential for driving growth and achieving success. Our team draws inspiration from several key books that guide our approach to business management and team building. Here are our top three must-reads:

Traction by Gino Wickman

The foundation of our operational strategy is built on the principles outlined in “Traction” by Gino Wickman. This book introduces the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS), a comprehensive framework designed to help businesses achieve their goals by improving accountability, communication, and execution. By implementing EOS, we ensure that every team member is aligned with our vision, committed to measurable goals, and accountable for their roles. This process has been instrumental in driving our company and portfolio companies towards sustained growth and success.

The Ideal Team Player by Patrick Lencioni

Building a cohesive and high-performing team is critical to our success. “The Ideal Team Player” by Patrick Lencioni provides valuable insights into the traits that make up an effective team member. According to Lencioni, the ideal team player possesses three core values: Humble, Hungry, and Smart. At ScaleCo Capital, we strive to cultivate these values within our team:

Humble: We prioritize the success of the team over individual achievements.
Hungry: We are always eager to take on more responsibilities and go the extra mile.
Smart: We possess strong interpersonal skills, enabling us to work effectively with others.

By embracing these values, we foster a collaborative and driven work environment that propels us towards our goals.

The Outward Mindset by The Arbinger Institute

Understanding and adopting the right mindset is crucial for effective leadership and team dynamics. “The Outward Mindset” by The Arbinger Institute teaches us to look beyond our personal objectives and consider the needs and challenges of others. This approach encourages empathy, improves communication, and enhances problem-solving abilities within our organization. By shifting our focus outward, we build stronger relationships with our colleagues, clients, and partners, ultimately leading to better outcomes for everyone involved.

Conclusion

At ScaleCo Capital, these books are more than just reading material; they are integral to our philosophy and operational approach. By embracing the principles in “Traction,” “The Ideal Team Player,” and “The Outward Mindset,” we continue to drive growth and maintain a culture of empathy and accountability.

About ScaleCo

ScaleCo Capital is a Cleveland-based lower middle market private equity firm that focuses on control-oriented leveraged buyout and growth equity investments in fast-growing companies based primarily in the Great Lakes region. ScaleCo partners with companies in the verticals of business services, tech-enabled services, value added distribution and assembly, and training, compliance, and education sectors, bringing operational expertise and strategic resources to scale their growth potential and build long-term value.
