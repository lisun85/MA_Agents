FIVE STAR ENERGY SERVICES

Headquartered in Wisconsin, Five Star provides underground infrastructure design, engineering, and construction services to customers in the telecom, water and gas end markets. Previously founder-owned by the Klumb family, the Company operates across the U.S. serving both private and public customers.

Over the last several years, Five Star has grown rapidly and established itself as an industry leader known for its unparalleled commitment to safety, quality, and customer service. Customers rely on Five Star as an innovative partner for critical infrastructure needs.
