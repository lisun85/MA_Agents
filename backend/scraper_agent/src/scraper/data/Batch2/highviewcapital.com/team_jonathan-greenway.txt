Jonathan Greenway

Managing Director

PROFESSIONAL

Jonathan Greenway is a Managing Director at Highview Capital. Jonathan is responsible for leading value creation and transformation activity at Highview’s portfolio companies as well as sourcing, diligence and execution of new investments.

Prior to joining Highview, Jonathan was a Managing Director and Global Practice Leader at AlixPartners, a New York-based restructuring and advisory firm, where he led the consumer industry practice. He led transformations for private equity and public clients across consumer, retail, industrials, technology and other sectors. Earlier in his career, he worked in industry leadership roles at Johnson & Johnson, Albertsons and Avery Dennison. He began his career in consulting at A.T. Kearney.

EDUCATION

Jonathan holds a B.A. in Economics from Brown University and is at The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania in the General Management Program.

PERSONAL

Jonathan grew up in Hong Kong, has lived in a dozen cities and now lives in California with his wife and three children. He loves spending time with his family, friends and pets. In his spare time, Jonathan enjoys being in the mountains, hiking, snowboarding and mountain biking.
