John Ciancio

Senior Associate

PROFESSIONAL

John Ciancio is a Senior Associate at Highview Capital. John is responsible for deal sourcing and manages new investment opportunities.

Prior to joining Highview, John worked as a Director at Trivest Partners, a Miami-based private equity firm, where he focused on regional business development initiatives across multiple industries for numerous funds within the firm. Previously, John worked at Lion Equity Partners, a private equity firm focused on divestitures and special situation opportunities.

EDUCATION

John holds a B.S. in Finance from the University of Indiana.

PERSONAL

John grew up in and now resides in Chicago, IL. In his free time, John enjoys skiing, hiking with his Rhodesian Ridgeback (Lucky), hunting, golfing, spending time with his family, and challenging himself in many different martial arts (mainly jiu jitsu).
