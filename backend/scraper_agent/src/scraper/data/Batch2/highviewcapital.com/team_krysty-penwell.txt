Krysty Penwell Grey

Vice President

PROFESSIONAL

Krysty Penwell Grey is a Vice President at Highview Capital. Krysty is responsible for deal sourcing and manages new investment opportunities.

Prior to joining Highview, Krysty worked as a Vice President at CenterOak Partners, a Dallas-based private equity firm, where she focused on business development across business services, industrials, and consumer sectors and led the firm’s ESG initiatives. Previously, Krysty worked at Cambridge Associates, a global investment firm focused on constructing, managing, and overseeing portfolios for foundations, endowments, and private clients across asset classes.

EDUCATION

Krysty holds a B.A. in Applied Mathematics and Economics from Vanderbilt University.

PERSONAL

Krysty grew up in Milwaukee and now resides in Dallas. In her free time, Krysty enjoys traveling, cycling, yoga, trying the latest restaurant concept, and spending time with her dog.
