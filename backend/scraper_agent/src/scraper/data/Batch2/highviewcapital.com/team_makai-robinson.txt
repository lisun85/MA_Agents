Makai Robinson

Associate

PROFESSIONAL

Makai Robinson is an Associate at Highview Capital. Makai is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence, and execution of new investments. Prior to joining Highview, Makai was in investment banking at Goldman Sachs, where he sat on their Consumer Retail desk focused on mergers and acquisitions.

EDUCATION

Makai holds a B.A. in Economics with a concentration in Business from the University of Chicago where he was a four-year starter on the varsity football team.

PERSONAL

Makai grew up in Encino, CA and now resides in Santa Monica. In his free time, Makai enjoys spending time with his family, watching Sunday football, and trying his absolute best on the jiu-jitsu mat and golf course.
