Tracy McGregor

Senior Advisor

PROFESSIONAL

Tracy McGregor is Chief Financial Officer at Karlin Asset Management, where she leads the finance and administrative operations. Prior to joining Karlin in 2005, Tracy was a Director of Financial Analysis at Alschuler Grossman Stein & Kahan LLC (AGSK), where she was principally responsible for overseeing the budgeting and forecasting functions for the firm. Before joining AGSK in 2002, Tracy was a Manager at Arthur Andersen LLC for nine years.


EDUCATION

Tracy is a graduate of the University of Southern California (B.S. in Accounting) and is a Certified Public Accountant.

PERSONAL

Tracy grew up in Lompoc, California, a small town on the central coast which has become a popular wine region where she visits often to relax and sip wine.
