Victor Bunce

Vice President

PROFESSIONAL

Victor Bunce is a Vice President at Highview Capital. Victor is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence and execution of new investments. Victor currently serves on the Board of B&R Auto, Watterson and Randall Foods.

Prior to joining Highview, Victor was in investment banking at Harris Williams, where he focused on mergers and acquisitions in the consumer sector.

EDUCATION

Victor holds a M.A. in Finance and a B.A. in Economics from Claremont McKenna College, where he graduated as a Robert Day Scholar. While at Claremont McKenna, Victor was a three-time All-American defensive back for the varsity football team and an executive in the college’s Student Investment Fund.

PERSONAL

Victor grew up outside of Sacramento and now resides in Manhattan Beach. In his free time, he enjoys all competitive games, beach volleyball, snowboarding, and his book club. He is a permanent believer in Iowa Hawkeye and San Francisco 49er football. He also spoils many good walks on the golf course.
