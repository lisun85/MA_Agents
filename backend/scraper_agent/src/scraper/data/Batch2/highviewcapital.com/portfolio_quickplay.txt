QUICKPLAY

Quickplay is a leading OTT video distribution platform for premium video to IP-connected devices. Following the acquisition of Quickplay from AT&T, Quickplay brings to market an enterprise grade technology solution that leverages the power of artificial intelligence to drive user engagement and monetization.

The company’s 5th generation, carrier grade solution can be deployed end-to-end or modularly to solve complex use cases. It powers millions of users, thousands of live channels, and manages video libraries from 300+ partners. Quickplay leverages artificial intelligence to drive user engagement and monetization for Tier 1 operators and content providers around the globe.
