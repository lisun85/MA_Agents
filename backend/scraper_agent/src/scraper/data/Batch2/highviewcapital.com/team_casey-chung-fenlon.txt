Casey Chung Fenlon

Senior Associate

PROFESSIONAL

Casey Chung Fenlon is a Senior Associate at Highview Capital. Casey is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence, and execution of new investments. Prior to joining Highview, Casey worked as an Associate at HarbourVest Partners, a global alternative asset manager based in Boston, where she focused on direct private co-investments. Casey previously worked at American Securities, a private equity firm based in New York, where she was an Associate on the investment team. Casey began her career at Credit Suisse investment bank in the M&A group.

EDUCATION

Casey holds a B.A. in Economics with a concentration in Value Investing from the University of California, Los Angeles.

PERSONAL

Casey grew up in Orange County, CA and now resides in Brentwood. In her free time, Casey enjoys pilates, experimental healthy baking (she only sometimes actually eats it), and hiking Runyon Canyon with her husband and their imaginary dog.
