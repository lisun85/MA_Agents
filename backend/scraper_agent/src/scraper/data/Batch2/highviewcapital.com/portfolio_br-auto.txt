B&R AUTO

B&R Auto is one of the nation’s largest independent auto recyclers serving mechanics, collision centers and other automotive clients with world class customer service.

For over 40 years, B&R has provided high quality recycled parts to automotive collision and repair centers and do-it-yourself customers. B&R operates over 20 locations across the Western U.S. focused on best-in-class dismantling processes, technology, and customer service.
