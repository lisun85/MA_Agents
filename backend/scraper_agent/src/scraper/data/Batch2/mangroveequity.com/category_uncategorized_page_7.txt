Mangrove Supports Computer Aided Technologies in the Acquisition of 3DVision Technologies

Posted September 6th, 2017 by Mangrove Equity Partners & filed under Portfolio News.

Mangrove Equity Partners is pleased to announce Computer Aided Technology, LLC’s (“CATI”) recent acquisition of 3DVision Technologies, LLC, expanding the Company’s footprint into Ohio, Michigan and Kentucky. This strengthens the Company’s position as one of the nation’s largest value added resellers of SolidWorks 3D computer aided design software and Stratasys 3D printers.
