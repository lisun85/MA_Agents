Jerry Neisel
Chief Compliance Officer & Treasurer

Jerry is Chief Compliance Officer & Treasurer at Kainos with over 20 years of private equity accounting and compliance experience. He is primarily responsible for the firm’s compliance procedures and operational administration.

 

Prior to joining Hicks, Muse, Tate and Furst in 1997, Jerry worked in financial planning for American Airlines for four years and as a senior accountant at KPMG in their audit practice for three years.

 

Jerry received his B.B.A. in Accounting from Baylor University and is a Certified Public Accountant.
