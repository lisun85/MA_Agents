Cate Mason
Associate

Cate Mason is an associate at Kainos Capital. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2023, Cate was an analyst in the Power, Energy, and Infrastructure Group at Lazard, where she focused on M&A advisory, capital raises, and restructuring.

 

Cate graduated summa cum laude from Southern Methodist University with a B.B.A. in Finance, a specialization in Alternative Asset Management, and a minor in Art History.
