Andrew Rosen
Managing Partner

Andrew Rosen is Managing Partner and Founder of Kainos Capital. Andrew brings over 25 years of investment experience in the food and consumer products sectors to Kainos. 

 

Prior to founding Kainos, Andrew joined Hicks, Muse, Tate and Furst (“HM”) in 1993, where he helped establish and lead the U.S. food and consumer products group. During his tenure at HM, the firm became an active acquirer of food assets. Prior to joining HM, Andrew was an associate with The Carlyle Group. 

 

Andrew is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania, receiving a B.A. in History, and the Wharton School of Business, receiving a B.S. in Economics.
