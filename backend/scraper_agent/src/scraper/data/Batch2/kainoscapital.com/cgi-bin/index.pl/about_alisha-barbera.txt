Alisha Barbera
Corporate Controller

Alisha is a corporate controller with over 20 years of accounting experience, mostly working with the Kainos team.  

 

Prior to working with the Kainos team members at Hicks, Muse (“HM”), she was an accountant at the law firm of Carrington, Coleman, Sloman & Blumenthal, LLP for two years.

 

Alisha received her B.S. in Accounting from the University of North Texas in 1994. She is a Certified Public Accountant and a member of the Texas Society and American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.
