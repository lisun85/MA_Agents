Steven Koch
Principal

Steven is a principal at Kainos with a focus on evaluating, executing, and managing investment opportunities in the food and consumer products sectors. 

 

Steven first joined Kainos in 2014 as an associate and rejoined in 2019. Prior to joining Kainos, he began his career as an investment banker with UBS Investment Bank’s Mergers & Acquisitions Group and Financial Institutions Group in New York City.

 

Steven holds an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern, and graduated with distinction from the University of Virginia with a B.S. in Commerce.
