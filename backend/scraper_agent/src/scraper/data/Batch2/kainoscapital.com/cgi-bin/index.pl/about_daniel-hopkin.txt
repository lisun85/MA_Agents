Daniel Hopkin
Partner

Dan is a partner of the firm and has over 15 years of investing and transaction experience in the food and consumer products sector. Dan focuses on evaluating, executing, and managing Kainos’ investment opportunities, and is a member of the firm’s investment committee.  

 

Prior to Kainos, Dan was a senior member of the food and consumer group at HM, having joined that team in 2004. He started his career in the Mergers & Acquisitions department of Morgan Stanley in New York.  

 

Dan received his B.A. in Accounting and his Master of Professional Accountancy from Brigham Young University. Dan is active with The Hopkin Family Society, his family foundation, which sponsors approximately 10 college students annually, primarily from Africa and South America.
