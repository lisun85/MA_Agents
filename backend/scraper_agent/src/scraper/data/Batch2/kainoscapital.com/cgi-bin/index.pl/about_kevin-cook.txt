Kevin Cook
Associate

Kevin Cook is an associate at Kainos with a focus on evaluating, executing and monitoring investment opportunities. He enjoys partnering with management teams to identify and execute on strategic initiatives that improve operational efficiency and propel long-term growth.

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2024, he was an associate at Intrepid Financial Partners in Houston. Prior to that, he was employed as an analyst in the Global Energy & Transition Group at Credit Suisse in Houston and began his career at Goldman Sachs in Salt Lake City.

 

Kevin graduated from the McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas with a B.B.A. in Finance and a minor in Accounting.
