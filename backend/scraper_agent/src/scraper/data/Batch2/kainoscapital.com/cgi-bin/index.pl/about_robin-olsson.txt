Robin Olsson
Managing Director

Robin is a managing director at Kainos with a focus on maximizing the operational performance of portfolio companies. He is focused on continuous improvement and is a Lean guru and a Six Sigma Black Belt.

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2017, he worked with Operations Strategy Partners LLC as a principal, providing operational improvement consulting services. Prior to that role, Robin spent 10 years at USC Consulting Group as a Senior Consultant and Senior Project Manager. Robin holds an APICS Certification in Production and Inventory Management (CPIM).

 

Robin holds a B.S. in Business Administration from Long Island University, where he graduated magna cum laude.
