Gage Baumli
Associate

Gage is an associate at Kainos Capital.  

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2023, Gage was an analyst in the Energy & Power Group at Wells Fargo in Houston, where he focused on M&A advisory and capital markets financing.

 

Gage graduated from Southern Methodist University with a B.B.A. in Finance and a specialization in Alternative Asset Management.
