Vineet Varma
Partner
San Francisco

Vineet joined Hanover in 2021 and is a Partner. Formerly he was a Director of Powertronix Holdings, LLC. Vineet has over a decade of experience in private equity and mergers & acquisitions, with a particular focus on industrial manufacturing. Prior to joining Hanover, Vineet was with Alvarez & Marsal Capital Partners and Industrial Growth Partners, where he focused primarily on investing in niche manufacturing companies. He started his career in the industrials group of the investment banking division of Citi. Vineet received an MBA from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and a BS in Electrical Engineering from UCLA.
