ABOUT

Headquartered in Charlotte, MI, Record Connect provides outsourced release of information (“ROI”) services to hospitals and other health care service providers across the Midwestern US.  Record Connect manages third-party requests for patient health care information (“PHI”) in a manner compliant with state and federal regulations.
