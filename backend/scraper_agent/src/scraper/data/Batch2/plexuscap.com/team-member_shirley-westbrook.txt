Shirley Westbrook
CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER EMERITUS
BIOGRAPHY

Shirley Westbrook is the Chief Administrative Officer Emeritus of Plexus Capital. She began working with Bob Anders in 1990 when she joined First Charlotte Bank and also worked with Mike Becker, Robert Gefaell, Kel Landis, and Michael Painter while at RBC Centura from 1993 to 2005.

Shirley formally retired in 2015 but will continue her work with Plexus on a consulting basis and as a permanent Plexus family member.
