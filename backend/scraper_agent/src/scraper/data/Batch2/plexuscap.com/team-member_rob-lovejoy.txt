Rob Lovejoy
VICE PRESIDENT
BIOGRAPHY

Rob joined Plexus in 2017 and is a Vice President on the investment team. Rob focuses primarily on due diligence, deal execution, and portfolio oversight.

Prior to joining Plexus, Rob played professional soccer for the Houston Dynamo where he was selected in the second round of the 2015 Major League Soccer draft.

Rob received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and was a letterman on the varsity soccer team.
