ABOUT

Headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio, Arc Drilling is a leading provider of fast hole electrical discharge machining (“EDM”), wire EDM, and sinker EDM manufacturing services. Arc Drilling was founded in 1961 and is a long-time supplier to OEMs and Tier I suppliers in the industrial gas turbine, aerospace engine, and industrial markets. Arc Drilling has manufacturing operations in Cleveland, Ohio; Euclid, Ohio; Greenville, South Carolina; Charleston, South Carolina; Hampton, VA; and Dover, NJ.
