Rachel Nachtrab
EXECUTIVE ASSISTANT
BIOGRAPHY

Rachel joined Plexus in April 2023 as an Executive Assistant to support the Deal Execution Team. Prior to Plexus, Rachel was an Administrative Associate at Longfellow Real Estate Partners where she supported the CEO and Partner of Real Estate Operations. She also managed the North Carolina Corporate office and supported initiatives such as corporate giving and investor tours.

Rachel is a graduate of North Carolina State University where she earned her Bachelor of Science in Business Management with a concentration in Agriculture.
