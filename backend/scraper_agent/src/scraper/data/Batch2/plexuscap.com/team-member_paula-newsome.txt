Paula Newsome
FUND ACCOUNTANT
BIOGRAPHY

Paula joined Plexus Capital in the Fall of 2012 and is the Fund Accountant. She focuses on the funds’ accounting and cash management.
Paula’s career began in Washington, D.C. working with Booz Allen Hamilton. Prior to working for Plexus Capital, Paula worked in the consulting industry for over 10 years.

Paula is a graduate of North Carolina State University where she earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration.
