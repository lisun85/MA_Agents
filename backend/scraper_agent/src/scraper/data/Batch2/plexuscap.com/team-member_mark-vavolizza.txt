BIOGRAPHY

Mark joined Plexus in 2020 and is a Vice President on the investment team. Mark focuses on sourcing investment opportunities, structuring transactions, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring. Mark began his career as an Analyst and then Associate in the Healthcare Services Investment Banking Group at Wells Fargo Securities providing M&A advisory and capital markets solutions to healthcare clients.

Mark is a graduate of the University of North Carolina where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration.
