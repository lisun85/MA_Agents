Racheal Adams to participate in FDA’s Compounding Quality Center of Excellence Virtual Conference

SCA Pharma Director of Corporate Quality Racheal Adams, will join leading industry experts for the FDA’s 2021 Compounding Quality Center of Excellence Virtual Conference titled “Culture of Quality” being held September 13-15.

The virtual conference will bring together outsourcing facilities, regulators and industry leadership to engage on emerging industry topics and best practices to improve the overall quality of compounded drugs, with a focus on culture of quality.

Racheal will participate in the panel discussion, “Cross-Sector Panel: Outsourcing Facilities and Purchasers” and is scheduled for Sept. 15 at 11:00 a.m. EDT.
