Excellere Partners Announces Key Promotions: Mike Forsyth Elevated to Principal and Ian Beck to Vice President of Origination

Denver, CO – January 14, 2025 – Excellere Partners, a leading private equity firm specializing in partnering with entrepreneurs and management teams to build enduring value, is pleased to announce the promotions of Mike Forsyth to Principal and Ian Beck to Vice President of Origination.

Mike Forsyth, who joined Excellere in 2016, has been instrumental in driving the firm’s investment strategies within the healthcare, industrial growth, and business services sectors. His strategic insights, analytical acumen, and leadership have significantly contributed to the growth and success of the firm and its portfolio companies.

Ian Beck, with Excellere since 2023, has played a pivotal role in sourcing and developing new investment opportunities during the last fifteen months. He will continue to drive the identification and pursuit of partnership opportunities with rapidly growing businesses that maintain durable value propositions and substantial competitive differentiation.

“We are thrilled to recognize the exceptional contributions of Mike and Ian with these well-deserved promotions,” said Brad Cornell. Managing Partner at Excellere Partners. “We are confident that they will continue to drive our mission of building industry leaders alongside our management team partners in their new roles.”

About Excellere Partners
Excellere Partners is a Denver-based private equity firm with $2.3 billion of committed capital across four funds that specialize in partnering with entrepreneurs and management teams through growth recapitalizations and management buyouts.  The firm employs a research-driven, top-down investment strategy and supports its entrepreneurs and management teams with a proprietary value-creation process designed to enhance the corporate and operational infrastructure for scalability and growth.  Excellere’s investments are focused on emerging growth companies positioned to benefit from industry consolidation and favorable macroeconomic and demographic trends.  The Firm’s targeted industry sectors include healthcare, industrial growth, and business services.  For more information about Excellere, please visit https://www.excellere.com.
