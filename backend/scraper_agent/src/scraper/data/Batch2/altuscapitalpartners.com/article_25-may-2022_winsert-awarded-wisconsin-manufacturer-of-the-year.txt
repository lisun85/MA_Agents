Winsert Awarded Wisconsin Manufacturer of the Year

Winsert Awarded Wisconsin Manufacturer of the Year

Company recognized for its innovation and commitment to customer satisfaction and job creation in their communities

May 23, 2022 – Milwaukee, WI – Winsert earned the illustrious Wisconsin Manufacturer of the Year award in the medium-sized company category. In its 33rd year, the annual Manufacturer of the Year Awards Program celebrates the successes of the manufacturer’s innovations, philanthropy, technological advances, commitment to customer satisfaction, and the quality jobs created in their communities. The award categories were broken down by company size, and the winners were selected by an independent panel of judges. This is the second time that Winsert has been honored with this award since 2018.

Mark Coduti, Winsert’s President, said, “Winning this award is a great honor. In addition, it formally recognizes two key focus points for Winsert - culture and team. Our culture fosters the proper environment for success and our fantastic team members make it happen. We are very proud of our accomplishments and appreciate the independent judges for recognizing it.”

Jason Anderson, Winsert’s Director of Sales & Marketing, stated, “This award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our employees who do what is necessary every day to make sure our customers are taken care of. This is a well deserved award for our employees, and we as an organization, appreciate the recognition.”

Winsert’s Director of Operations, Tom Woods, was quoted as saying, “Receiving this prestigious award is a fantastic achievement. Our recognition for such a respected award is attributed to the efforts and commitment of all the employees.”

Winsert is a global provider of innovative alloy solutions. In addition to manufacturing components in traditional common trade materials, Winsert utilizes patented and proprietary alloys to develop fast, effective, and affordable solutions for their OEM partners. Winsert's alloys serve as true replacement materials for expensive high cobalt and nickel content materials. With the everfluctuating metal markets and cobalt instability, Winsert’s patented and proprietary alloys provide peace of mind in terms of proven performance as well as cost savings and cost stability. Winsert provides corrosion-resistant, wear-resistant, and high temperature alloys for various applications within internal combustion engines, power generation, emission controls, industrial valves, and food processing. For more information on Winsert, please visit www.winsert.com.
