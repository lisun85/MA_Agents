Laurie Siegel

Office Manager & Client Relations

Laurie Siegel joined Altus Capital Partners in 2021 as the Firm’s Office Manager. Laurie's responsibilities include regulatory matters, the Firm’s website including the investor portal, general marketing, all vendor relationships, facilitating Annual Investor Meetings, Portfolio Company Summits and general office administration.

Laurie has a unique blend of classical corporate and entrepreneurial environment experience. Acting as a “chief of staff” for advertising agency senior executives, she assisted in the development and implementation of integrated marketing plans for venture capital backed startups as well as high profile consumer package goods companies including Kraft, Unilever, Campbell Soup and Motts. Additionally, in her administrative and operations roles, she has developed expertise navigating through state and federal regulatory agency requirements.

 

Laurie earned a BA Degree from the Lubin School of Business, Pace University.
