Altus Capital Partners, Inc. Names Nick deMarco as Principal and Joshua Tesoriero as Vice President

Wilton, CT, June 25, 2019 – Altus Capital Partners, Inc. (“Altus”), an investment firm focused on highly engineered middle market manufacturing companies, today announced it has promoted Nick deMarco to Principal and Joshua Tesoriero to Vice President. Messrs. deMarco and Tesoriero will be based in the Wilton office.

As Principal, Mr. deMarco will be responsible for deal sourcing, strategic analysis, market research, underwriting, due diligence, closing new investments and assisting in managing existing portfolios. He joined Altus Capital Partners in 2016. Prior to Altus, Mr. deMarco held a number of positions at GE where most recently he was Assistant Vice President of GE Capital. Mr. deMarco started his career at GE Aircraft Engines where he had multiple roles in engineering, manufacturing, audit, and finance.

A CFA Charterholder, Mr. deMarco earned an MBA with honors and distinction from Columbia Business School and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from M.I.T.

In his expanded role as Vice President, Mr. Tesoriero will be responsible for investment due diligence, underwriting, and portfolio company monitoring. He began his career at Morgan Stanley & Co. in a business strategy and analytics role. He holds an MBA and Master of International Affairs from Columbia University and an AB from Harvard University.

Russell Greenberg, Managing Partner of Altus Capital Partners, said, “Altus Capital continues to see an increase in its investment activities. We attribute our success to the availability of high quality professionals on our team, and look forward to Nick and Josh’s continued contributions to the firm and its investors.”

About Altus Capital Partners

Altus Capital Partners is a private equity firm that makes control investments in middle market manufacturing businesses. We believe that our exclusive focus on manufacturing provides us with a unique understanding of the opportunities and challenges faced by companies in the sector.
We utilize a patient, thoughtful investment approach and seek to partner with the management teams of our operating companies to achieve growth.  For more information, please visit www.altuscapitalpartners.com/.
