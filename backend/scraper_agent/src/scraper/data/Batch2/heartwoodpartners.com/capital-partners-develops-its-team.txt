TITLE: Capital Partners Develops Its Team - Heartwood Partners

BASE CONTENT:
================================================================================
In recognition of their strong performance and reaching targeted development goals, Capital Partners has promoted three executives, with Anastasia Kowal becoming Chief Financial Officer and Mark Langer and James Sidwa becoming Managing Directors. Capital Partners will continue to build and develop its team and is presently searching for Associates, Senior Associates and Operating Professionals.

Capital Partners and its portfolio company management teams are focused on continued growth through new platform investments, organic initiatives and add-on acquisitions. Our platform investment criteria and a description of our portfolio companies may be found at (www.capitalpartners.com).

About Capital Partners

Capital Partners (www.capitalpartners.com) is a private investment firm founded in 1982. Our principals have invested in more than 90 platform and add-on acquisitions across a variety of industries. Based on the combination of solid investment discipline and our low leverage approach, we have successfully partnered with management teams through multiple economic cycles.
