Protect Environmental (“Protect”) is a radon mitigation and water treatment provider, serving residential consumers and commercial customers. Protect performs radon testing, design and installation of radon mitigation systems, and residential water testing and system installation. Within radon mitigation, Protect provides high quality service and consultation at scale to customers throughout the United States. Through its broad service capabilities and proprietary testing products, Protect is a trusted provider to home owners, real estate developers, real estate agents, and property owners.
