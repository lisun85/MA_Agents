Craftsman Custom Metals (CCM), located in Schiller Park, Illinois, is a full-service provider of precision metal components and products, with expertise in optimized design, prototyping, and manufacture and assembly capabilities. CCM offers custom manufacturing with a focus on simplified processes, rapid response, and total supply chain management. The company’s lean, cellular manufacturing approach gives its customers more flexibility and greater control, resulting in superior quality products, highly efficient production, and elevated value for CCM’s customers.
