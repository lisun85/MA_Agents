Western Industries, Inc. (“Western”) has more than 50 years of experience in manufacturing innovations. Western is a contract manufacturer capable of providing individual components as well as complete, custom-designed appliance products, electro-mechanical assemblies, and complex cosmetic assemblies. Western’s fully integrated manufacturing facility incorporates linked processes of metal stamping, metal fabrication, welding (MIG, TIG, Resistance, and Robotic), paint (epoxy e-coat, wet topcoat), metal finishing, and assembly that provides efficiency and offers increased control.
