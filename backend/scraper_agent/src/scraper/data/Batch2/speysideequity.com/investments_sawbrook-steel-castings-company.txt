Sawbrook Steel Castings (“Sawbrook”), founded in 1924 and based in Cincinnati, Ohio, produces low-carbon, low-alloy steel castings for numerous industries including military, mining, and construction. Sawbrook is capable of producing steel castings ranging from one to 10,000 pounds of net casting weight.
