Additional Investment in syncreon Announced by GenNx360 Capital Partners
Portfolio Company News - November 17, 2011

GenNx360 Capital Partners, L.P., a middle market, operationally-focused private equity firm, announced today that it increased its investment in and ownership of syncreon, a global asset-light logistics and supply chain solutions portfolio company. This marks GenNx360's third increase in its investment in syncreon since its original investment in February, 2009.

"We are excited about this opportunity to further increase our commitment to syncreon. The stability of the business, its attractive growth dynamics, long term and well established customer relationships, and experienced management team makes this a very attractive investment," said Lloyd Trotter, a GenNx360 Founder and Managing Partner.

This latest investment in syncreon gives GenNx360 majority control of the company. To complete this investment, capital was provided from both its fund and affiliates of GenNx360.

"This is very much business as usual for syncreon. GenNx360 has been a long term investor in syncreon and their continued investment in syncreon represents their confidence in syncreon's strategy, management and business model. The Enright family is excited about continuing their relationship with GenNx360 and growing the business together," said Brian Enright, CEO of syncreon.

About GenNx360 Capital Partners

GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity 3.0 firm that applies leading operational expertise and investment discipline to successfully buy, guide, grow and sell industrial B2B companies, generate superior returns, and preserve capital regardless of market cycles.
