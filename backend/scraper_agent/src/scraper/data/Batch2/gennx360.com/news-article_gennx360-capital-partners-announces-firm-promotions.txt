GenNx360 Capital Partners Announces Firm Promotions
Portfolio Company News - November 03, 2009

GenNx360 Capital Partners, a private equity firm focused on middle market opportunities, announced today the following organization changes:

 

Rena Clark has been promoted to Partner of the firm. As a Partner she will be able to contribute at a high level to sourcing and implementing transactions;
Carmen Rojas has been promoted to Investor Relations Manager;
Kimberly Berry-Jeter has been promoted to Office Manager for our New York Headquarters.

 

About GenNx360 Capital Partners GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity 3.0 firm that applies leading operational expertise and investment discipline to successfully buy, guide, grow and sell industrial B2B companies, generate superior returns and preserve capital regardless of market cycles.
