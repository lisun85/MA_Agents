Anthony Willis Joins GenNx360 Capital Partners as Operating Associate
Portfolio Company News - December 10, 2010

GenNx360 Capital Partners, a private equity firm focused on industrial business-to-business companies with $50 million to $500 million in revenues, announced today that it has hired Anthony Willis as operating associate.

Mr. Willis will primarily be focused on operational support for GenNx360 transactions, third-party engagements and supporting the firm's portfolio companies. He reports to senior partner Charles Castine.

Mr. Willis' experience includes assignments at private equity firm Silverline Partners, as well as the New York City Retirement Systems (NYCERS), where he worked with private equity and real estate investment programs to manage and grow $12.5 billion and $5.4 billion portfolios, respectively.

Mr. Willis earned a MBA from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and a MS degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Central Florida, where he served as captain and three-time letterman of the university's football team. He was also on the Conference USA Honor Roll and received the National Student Athlete award.

About GenNx360 Capital Partners

GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity 3.0 firm that applies leading operational expertise and investment discipline to successfully buy, guide, grow and sell industrial B2B companies, generate superior returns and preserve capital regardless of market cycles.
