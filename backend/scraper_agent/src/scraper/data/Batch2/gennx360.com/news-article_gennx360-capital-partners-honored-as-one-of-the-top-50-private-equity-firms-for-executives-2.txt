GenNx360 Capital Partners Honored as One of the Top 50 Private Equity Firms for Executives
Portfolio Company News - March 15, 2024
New York, New York.  March 15, 2024 – GenNx360 Capital Partners (“GenNx360”) is proud to be announced as one of 2024’s Top 50 Private Equity Firms for Executives for the second consecutive year.  The annual list is a collaboration between PrivateEquityCXO (“PE-CXO”) and Falcon Partners. PE-CXO is the world’s largest community of private equity-backed executives. Falcon Partners is a leading retained executive search firm exclusively serving private equity firms and their portfolio companies.  The list highlights the funds whose governance style, engagement, and fit are seen as best enabling their executive partners to achieve success. Funds are rated on the Nine Dimensions of Governance Fit®, a strategic framework that guides executives and sponsors in achieving optimal partnerships more likely to result in higher ROI.
 
As part of the selection process,  more than 1,000 sponsor-backed executives were surveyed and interviewed. Participating executives were selected for their credible track record and exposure to more than one sponsor. Key criteria included governance, management’s desire to stay, and value-add support to executives.  Fund and investment performance were also important considerations. 
 
“We are honored to be named as a Top 50 Private Equity Firm for Executives and recognized for the continued achievements of our team.  From its inception, GenNx360 has served as a partner to our portfolio management teams and our investors. This recognition honors our continued focus on integrity, governance, and engagement to achieve success and value creation for those we serve.” said Ron Blaylock, GenNx360 Founder and Managing Partner.
 
For the complete list of top 50 Private Equity Firms for Executives, please visit www.privateequitycxo.com/top-50-firms
 
About GenNx360 Capital Partners
GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity firm focused on investing in middle market business services and industrial companies. GenNx360 partners with companies having proven and sustainable business models in expanding industries, with the objective of implementing and supporting value enhancing organic and inorganic initiatives to accelerate growth, deliver efficiencies and generate strong financial returns. GenNx360 was founded in 2006 and is headquartered in New York City. For more information, please visit www.gennx360.com.
