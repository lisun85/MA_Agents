ARBITER

https://arbiter.io/

Sandy, Utah

Arbiter is a trusted leader in athletic and event management, providing schools, athletic organizations, and state associations with the tools they need to manage registration, payments, scheduling, assigning, and communication seamlessly. For over 40 years, Arbiter has helped administrators, athletic directors, assigners, officials, and event staff do their jobs effectively by offering reliable solutions that improve coordination and simplify workflows.
With a secure, web-based platform, Arbiter delivers the control and visibility organizations need to ensure events run smoothly. Thousands of schools and organizations rely on Arbiter to support them in creating impactful experiences for students, families, and communities.
