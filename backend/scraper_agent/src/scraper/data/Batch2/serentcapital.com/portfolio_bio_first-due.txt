FIRST DUE

https://www.firstdue.com/

New York, NY

New York-based First Due has reimagined public safety technology for agencies nationwide. The SaaS First Due Suite provides fire and EMS agencies an end-to-end solution to consolidate all core components of their operation in a single platform.

 

Departments with First Due are transforming how they function, before, during and after an incident – ultimately leading to better decisions and a safer community.
