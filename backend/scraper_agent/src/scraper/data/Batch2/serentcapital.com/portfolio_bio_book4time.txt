BOOK4TIME

https://book4time.com/

Markham, ON

Book4Time is a global leader in wellness and recreation management solutions for the hospitality market. Founded in 2004, Book4Time manages the end-to-end guest experience for international hotels, resorts, casinos, and golf clubs. The company’s customers include world-renowned hospitality brands such as Marriott, Accor, Hilton, Four Seasons, Hyatt, Shangri-La, Caesars, Canyon Ranch, and Equinox. The system’s architecture is cloud-based and fully scalable for complete management control of multi-location businesses from a central online service platform and is used in over 85 countries globally.
