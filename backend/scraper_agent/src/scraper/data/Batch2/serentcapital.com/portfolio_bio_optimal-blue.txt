OPTIMAL BLUE

http://www.optimalblue.com/

Plano, TX

Optimal Blue is a leading provider of SaaS mortgage technology solutions. The company’s products include a web-based product eligibility and pricing engine (PPE), as well as compliance and hedging tools for mortgage originators. Founded in 2002, the company’s software suite provides lenders with the ability to automate the management and distribution of their products, pricing and secondary marketing activities.
