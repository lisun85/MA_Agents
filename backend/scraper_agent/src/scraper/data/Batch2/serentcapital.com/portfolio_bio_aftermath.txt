AFTERMATH

http://www.aftermath.com/

Aurora, IL

Aftermath is a leading provider of biohazard management, crime scene and trauma cleanup services, on a national scale. The Company also provides awareness training to law enforcement agencies, first responders, medical examiners, and other professionals to educate them on the hazards of bloodborne pathogens and other infectious diseases, as well as proper safety procedures.
