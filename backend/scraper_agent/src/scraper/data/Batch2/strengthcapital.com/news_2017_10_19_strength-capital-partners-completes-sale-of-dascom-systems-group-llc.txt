Strength Capital Partners Completes Sale of Dascom Systems Group, LLC

Birmingham-based Strength Capital Partners, LLC is pleased to announce the sale of Dascom Systems Group, LLC (“Dascom”).  

Dascom is the leading integrator of legacy and next-generation IPTV systems since 1995 with more than 200 telecommunications customers in the United States, Canada and the Caribbean.  The Company’s products and services include headend integration, Over-the-Top (OTT) video, IPTV, middleware, local video content offerings and production, Video-on-Demand (VoD) and adaptive video streaming.  

Strength Capital acquired Dascom in 2006.
