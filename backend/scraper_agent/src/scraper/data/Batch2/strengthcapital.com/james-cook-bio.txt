About James

As an Associate at Strength Capital, James focuses primarily on deal due diligence, portfolio monitoring, and project work.

Prior to joining Strength Capital, James worked as a consultant at Palm Tree LLC, focusing primarily on strategic finance and accounting advisory engagements for private equity clients. James also worked in the Transaction Advisory Services group at Doeren Mayhew, and, prior to that, started his career in Grant Thornton’s Audit Practice. He is a Certified Public Accountant.

James attended the Eli Broad College of Business at Michigan State University, obtaining both a Bachelors and Masters of Accounting. Outside of work, he enjoys improving his golf game, supporting local Detroit and Michigan State sports teams, and spending time with his wife, Ashley.
