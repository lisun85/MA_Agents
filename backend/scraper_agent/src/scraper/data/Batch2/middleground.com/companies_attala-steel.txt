Attala Steel Industries is a leading manufacturer and distributor of specialty steel foundation components for ground-mounted utility-scale solar PV installations. The Company is vertically integrated with milling, fabrication and galvanizing capabilities, enabling Attala to offer customers high quality, low cost materials with a rapid response time and on-time delivery. Attala’s product offering includes galvanized I-beam posts, mounting rack supports, galvanized H-pilings and driven pier structures.
