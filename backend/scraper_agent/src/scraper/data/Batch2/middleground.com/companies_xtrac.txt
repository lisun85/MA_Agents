Xtrac is the leading global supplier of professional motorsport and specialized high-performance automotive transmission systems across internal combustion engine, hybrid, and electric vehicles, providing in-house manufacturing as well as design and engineering services. Xtrac’s focus on performance and reliability has made them the benchmark in the industry, testified by their longstanding supply to a large number of the world’s top professional race teams and organizations, including teams in Formula One, Formula E, Sportscar and touring car racing globally, world rallying and off-road racing, IndyCar and NASCAR.
