TPM is a market leader in bespoke thermoplastic, thermoset injection molding that was established in 1947. With over 150 injection molding machine and secondary equipment including; vibration, ultrasonic, hot plate welding, leak decay, pressure, and flow testing, TPM can mold individual components or complex sub and final assemblies. Their capabilities include thermoplastic and thermoset molding, insert, 2-shot and over-molding, decorative, assembly, and test.

TPM enables customers to resource tooling at an expedited timeline, protects their supply chain, allows the ability to fine-tune new tooling, and undertakes routine preventative maintenance.
