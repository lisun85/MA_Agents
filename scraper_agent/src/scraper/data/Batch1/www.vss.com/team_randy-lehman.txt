Randy Lehman
CTO/Director of Technology

Randy Lehman is the CTO/Director of Technology and joined VSS in 1997.  He is responsible for all facets of technology within the firm including infrastructure, cloud, technical operations, support, cybersecurity, and project management.

Randy holds an MS from Columbia University in Technology Management and a BS from Marist College in Computer Science.
