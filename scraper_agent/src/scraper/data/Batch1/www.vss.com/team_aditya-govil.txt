Our Team
Aditya Govil
Vice President

Aditya Govil is a Vice President and joined VSS in 2021. He focuses on sourcing, evaluating, executing, and monitoring investments. Aditya is currently a board member of the following VSS portfolio companies – GLOBO Language Solutions, Specialty1 Partners, Ambulatory Anesthesia Care, Olympus Cosmetic Group and Center for Rheumatology.

Prior to joining VSS, he was a Venture Capital Investor at Alumni Ventures Group and prior to that, he was an Operator and Consultant at Avant-garde Health (Sponsor-backed company), the Advisory Board Company (part of UnitedHealth Group, a Fortune 10 company), and Strata (part of Roper Technologies, a Fortune 1000 and S&P 500 company).

Aditya holds an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and BS in Computer Science and Mathematics (minor) from the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor.
