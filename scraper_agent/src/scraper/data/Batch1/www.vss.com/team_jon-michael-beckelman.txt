Our Team
Jon-Michael Beckelman
Senior Associate

Jon-Michael Beckelman is a Senior Associate and joined VSS in 2023. He is responsible for evaluating, executing, and monitoring investments. Prior to joining VSS, Jon-Michael was an investment banking associate at Leerink Partners focused exclusively on healthcare M&A advisory. He began his career as an investment banking analyst in the Healthcare Group at RBC Capital Markets.

Jon-Michael holds an MBA from Columbia Business School and a BS in Finance and Accounting from Lehigh University.
