Ella Opatzevsky
Tax Director

Ella Opatzevsky is the Tax Director and joined VSS in 2010. She oversees the tax compliance process for all VSS funds, working closely with the VSS finance department and public accounts.

Prior to joining VSS, Ella worked as a Tax Manager for PricewaterhouseCoopers, LLP.

Ella holds a BBA in accounting from Baruch College.
