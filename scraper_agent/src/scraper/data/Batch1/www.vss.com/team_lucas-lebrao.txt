Our Team
Lucas Lebrao
Senior Associate

Lucas Lebrao is a Senior Associate and joined the firm in 2021. He is responsible for evaluating, executing, and monitoring investments.

Prior to joining VSS, Lucas was an investment banking analyst in Houlihan Lokey's business services group focusing exclusively on M&A transactions. Prior to Houlihan Lokey, he was an investment banking analyst at Provident Healthcare Partners, a boutique investment bank specializing in healthcare M&A advisory. Lucas began his career at Trinity Partners as an analytics and strategy consultant.

Lucas holds a BS in Finance from Bentley University.
