COLE MORRISON
Position: AssociateCategories: team

Mr. Morrison is involved in the sourcing, acquisition and oversight of new and existing investments. Prior to joining Branford in 2023, Mr. Morrison worked as an investment banking associate in the Private Capital Advisory Group at Harris Williams (previously known as Sixpoint Partners), where he focused on providing bespoke liquidity solutions to private equity firms. Prior to Harris Williams, Mr. Morrison worked as an investment banking analyst at KeyBanc Capital Markets in the Building Products and Paper & Packaging Groups, where he focused on mergers and acquisitions as well as equity and debt financing transactions. Mr. Morrison graduated from Babson College with a B.S. in Business Administration with concentrations in Finance and Computational and Mathematical Finance.
