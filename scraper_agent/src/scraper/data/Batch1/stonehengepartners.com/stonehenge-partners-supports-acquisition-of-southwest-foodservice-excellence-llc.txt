Stonehenge Partners Supports Acquisition of Southwest Foodservice Excellence, LLC

September 2017:  Stonehenge Partners supported the a cquisition of Southwest Foodservice Excellence (“SFE” or the “Company”) by Harkness Capital Partners. Located in Scottsdale, Arizona, SFE  is a provider of outsourced foodservice management services to K-12 public schools located primarily in the Southwestern United States. 

The Company serves more than 80 million meals annually across more than 100 school districts in 11 states. SFE focuses on delivering nutritious and high quality food, providing exceptional service and more menu choices to meet specific needs of K-12 public school districts.
