================================================================================
Jim joined Blue Point as Controller in April 2018. Previously, he was an auditor at Cohen & Company, Ltd. within their Investment Industry Services Division. Prior to Cohen, he was a Senior Analyst at Citi Hedge Fund Services North America, Inc. providing fund administration and partnership accounting services to various types of private funds.

Jim graduated from Ohio University with a B.B.A. in Accounting. Since 2010, he has been a CPA licensed in Ohio.

Outside of work, Jim enjoys spending time with his wife and daughter, traveling, golfing and exploring the local food and brewery scene.
