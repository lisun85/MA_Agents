Blue Point is honored to have been named one of the nation’s top 50 Best Private Equity Firms for Entrepreneurs by Inc. Magazine.


Making this list is especially gratifying to Blue Point, who prides itself on developing close partnerships and strong connections with the founders and management teams at its portfolio companies.  

Inc. Magazine independently identified private equity firms that have invested in founder-led companies, gathered data on their portfolio companies’ growth and asked entrepreneurs to share their experiences while partnered with those firms. The research yielded a list of 50 firms with a track record of successfully backing entrepreneurs.
