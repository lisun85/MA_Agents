================================================================================
Charley joined the group in 2008. Prior to joining Blue Point, Charley worked as a Senior Associate for Duff & Phelps Securities. He began his career at Willis Towers Watson, working in its investment consulting group.

Charley holds a B.S.B.A. in Finance from the University of Richmond. He is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and a member of the CFA Institute and CFA Society of Cleveland. Charley serves as a director of Russell Hendrix, TAS Environmental and Weaver Leather.

Outside of the office, Charley enjoys spending time with his wife and two young children. He also loves sports and is a passionate supporter of his hometown teams in Cleveland.
