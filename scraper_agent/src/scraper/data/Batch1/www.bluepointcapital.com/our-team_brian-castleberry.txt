Brian rejoined the group in 2015. Most recently, Brian was a vice president at Red Ventures where he led the predictive analytics and advanced digital marketing teams. He has also worked with HOPE International in Brazzaville, Congo, and spent two years as an investment banking analyst with Harris Williams & Co., specializing in mergers and acquisitions.

Brian graduated from Washington and Lee University with a B.A. in Economics and Mathematics.

On behalf of Blue Point, Brian serves as a director of National OnDemand, SBG and Transtar.

Away from the office, Brian enjoys traveling, photography and fly fishing.
