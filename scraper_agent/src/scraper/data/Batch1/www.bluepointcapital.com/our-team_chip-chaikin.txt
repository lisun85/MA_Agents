================================================================================
Chip has been with the group since 1998 and established Blue Point’s global supply chain function in 2004. Prior to Blue Point, Chip worked in business development for the Sara Lee Corporation in Hong Kong, as well as for Strategic Planning Associates (now part of Oliver Wyman), providing strategic consulting services to a number of Fortune 500 companies.

Chip holds a B.A. in Asian Studies from Brown University and a Master’s of Public Affairs from Princeton University. Chip has been a contributor on issues related to China investments to a number of national news organizations, including Bloomberg, Reuters, The Wall Street Journal and American Public Media’s national radio show, Marketplace.

On behalf of Blue Point, Chip serves as a director of Next Level, siffron, Spector & Co., Stax and Transtar.

In his spare time, Chip enjoys biking, swimming and traveling with his family. He met his wife in the fifth grade and they have four kids, with music in their house running the gamut from Katy Perry to Kendrick Lamar. Chip also serves as a Court Appointed Special Advocate (CASA), working with children in the Cuyahoga County juvenile court system. He is also a former member of the Shanghai chapter of the Young Presidents' Organization.
