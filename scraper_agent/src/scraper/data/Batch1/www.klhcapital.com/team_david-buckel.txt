David Buckel
Partner

David works with KLH’s portfolio companies to evaluate, develop, and execute operational strategies that deliver continuous improvement and measurable results. He also serves as a resource for the portfolio companies to execute special projects, such as acquisition integration, geographic expansion, process improvement, and key employee recruitment.

David has over 25 years of leadership experience ranging from start-up ventures to public companies both globally and domestically across various industries. David has extensive experience creating accounting and control systems and processes, corporate strategizing, managing growth expansion, and integrating acquisitions from a systems perspective. He has served in executive positions for several private equity sponsored companies ranging from N3 Results in Atlanta, Georgia to Applied Theory Communications in Syracuse, New York. Most recently, David was CFO/CPO at Data Intensity. Prior to that, he served as President and Managing Director for BVI Venture Services.

David graduated from Canisius College with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and earned a Master of Business Administration in Operations Management and Finance from Syracuse University.
