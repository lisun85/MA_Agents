Nick is responsible for executing transactions, due diligence and financial analysis, and portfolio management. Nick graduated from Clemson University with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences and earned a Master of Science in Commerce in Finance from the University of Virginia’s McIntire School of Commerce.
