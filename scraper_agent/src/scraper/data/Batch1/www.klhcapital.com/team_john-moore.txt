John Moore
CFO & CCO

John is primarily responsible for managing all of KLH’s compliance and accounting activities. Prior to joining KLH, John served as the CFO and CCO of AUA Private Equity Partners, LLC, a lower middle market private equity firm targeting companies within the consumer products and services sectors. John is a seasoned finance professional, and has managed the delivery of administration services, among other finance functions at a variety of companies throughout his career. John graduated from Davidson College with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and earned a Master of Business Administration from the University of Rochester.
