Mr. McCown is a Principal of LCM and is responsible for sourcing, executing, and managing investments across healthcare industry sectors. He is currently a member of the Board of Directors of Institutes of Health.

Mr. McCown most recently served as Chief Financial Officer of Espire Dental, a fast growing dental services organization, where he led all M&A, finance and accounting functions. Prior to joining Espire, Mr. McCown was a senior private equity investment professional at KRG Capital Partners and Mountaingate Capital where he partnered with company founders and management teams across a variety of industries and served on the Board of Directors for several of the firms’ portfolio companies. Mr. McCown began his career as an investment banking Analyst with PJ Solomon Company in New York.

Mr. McCown earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from The University of Chicago.
