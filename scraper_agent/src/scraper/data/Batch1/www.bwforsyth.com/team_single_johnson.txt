ASHLEY JOHNSON
Executive Assistant

Ashley joined BW Forsyth Partners as Executive Assistant in December 2024, supporting our Partners and team in a variety of areas.

Previously, Ashley worked as an Administrative Assistant at Humana Inc, a healthcare insurance company, supporting their Vice President of Provider Network Management and direct reports. Ashley has multiple years of experience managing several leaders and coordinating in-person events. Before this, Ashley spent time working in the Sales department at the St. Louis Blues, which was the role that brought her to the St. Louis area.

Prior to launching her career, Ashley earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Sport Leadership and Management with a minor in Marketing from Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. During her time there, she also completed the Mini MBA Certification from Farmer School of Business.

In her free time, Ashley enjoys traveling, trying new restaurants, and visiting her family back in Ohio. Some of her favorite travels include Marco Island, Hawaii, and Florence, Italy.
