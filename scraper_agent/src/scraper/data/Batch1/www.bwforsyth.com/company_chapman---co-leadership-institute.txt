CHAPMAN & CO. LEADERSHIP INSTITUTE
St. Louis, MO
Acquired: October 2023

Chapman & Co. Leadership Institute helps clients select, develop, and retain high-performing team members who align with company values. Guided by a team of organizational psychologists and consultants, the team identifies current environment, gaps, and a tailored path forward rooted in human behavioral science. The comprehensive suite of services includes pre-hire & development assessments, executive coaching, and organizational development consulting. By optimizing talent strategies, Chapman & Co. creates an environment where people and organizations can thrive. Chapman & Co. has worked with more than 600 organizations across a variety of industries – including airlines, manufacturers, and grocery stores – to build strong cultures that drive strong businesses.

To learn more, visit ccoleadership.com.
