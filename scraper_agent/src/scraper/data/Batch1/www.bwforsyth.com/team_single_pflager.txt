BANKS PFLAGER
Associate

Banks joined the team in 2024 as an Associate, with responsibility for sourcing, evaluating, and executing M&A transactions and supporting our operating companies’ senior leadership teams on key strategic initiatives. 

Previously, Banks spent two years as an Analyst at J.P. Morgan in the Leveraged Finance Group where he participated in transactions in support of sponsor and strategic acquisitions, refinancing, and debt restructurings across a number of sectors.

Outside of the office, Banks enjoys traveling and exploring new cities, golfing, and cheering on St. Louis sports and the (formerly St. Louis) LA Rams.
