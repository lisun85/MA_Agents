Daniel’s primary role at HKW is to spearhead the firm’s Technology focus which includes evaluating and partnering with potential investment opportunities in the software and technology sector.

Prior to joining HKW, Daniel was one of the co-founding partners of Bregal Sagemount, a technology-focused growth capital investment firm. Early in his career, he held roles at Goldman, Sachs & Co.’s Americas Special Situations Group, JMI Equity, and Broadview International.

Daniel lives in New York, NY. Outside of the office, he supports a range of classical music organizations and enjoys taking culinary classes.
