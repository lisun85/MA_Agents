DESCRIPTION

Greenfiber is the largest North American producer of cellulose insulation, serving primarily the residential remodeling and new-home construction markets under its Sanctuary® brand. In addition to its robust thermal, acoustic, fire, and air insulation properties, cellulose insulation is a carbon negative, sustainable product that is made primarily from recycled materials, does not require harsh chemicals to produce, and contributes towards lower energy usage.

Greenfiber was formed through the simultaneous acquisitions of Applegate Holdings, LLC and Greenfiber, LLC.
