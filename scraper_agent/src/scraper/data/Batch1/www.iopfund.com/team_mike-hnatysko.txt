MIKE HNATYSKO

Vice President, Accounting and Compliance

> WITH IOP SINCE 2012

Mike has over 10 years of experience as a regulatory auditor and analyst in asset management firms. At IOP, he is responsible for accounting and compliance.

Prior to joining IOP, Mike served as a consultant reviewing accounting and operations groups for hedge fund of funds. Previously, he worked in portfolio management and operations groups at Glenwood Capital Investments LLC, focusing on hedge fund of fund liquidity and valuation.

Mike began his career at the Chicago Mercantile Exchange Inc. as an auditor after graduating from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign with a degree in accounting. He is a CPA (inactive).
