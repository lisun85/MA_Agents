GRACE RICHMOND

Vice President, Business Development

> WITH IOP SINCE 2021

Grace is a Business Development Vice President at IOP, responsible for supporting the investment sourcing process and developing the firm’s relationships with outside partners including investment banks, financing sources and other institutions.

Prior to joining IOP, Grace served as an Analyst in the Global Industrials Group at Citigroup Global Markets, where she focused on M&A advisory work.

Grace holds a B.A. in Econometrics and Quantitative Economics from Boston College.
