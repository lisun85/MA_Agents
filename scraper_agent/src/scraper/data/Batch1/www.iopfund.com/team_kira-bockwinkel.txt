KIRA BOCKWINKEL

Controller

> WITH IOP SINCE 2024

Kira has several years of experience in accounting, auditing and private fund management. At IOP, her responsibilities include accounting/tax, portfolio valuations, investor relations, banking and compliance.

Prior to joining IOP, Kira served as a controller at AJ Capital Partners where she oversaw both management company accounting and fund accounting for a portfolio of boutique hotels. Before joining AJ Capital Partners, Kira was an audit manager at KPMG, where she provided audit services to a diverse range of clients within the investment management and real estate sectors.

Kira earned a BBA in Accounting from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and is a licensed CPA.
