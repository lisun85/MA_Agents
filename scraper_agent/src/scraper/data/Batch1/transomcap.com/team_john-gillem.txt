JOHN GILLEM
Executive Vice President, Operations

As Executive Vice President of Operations, John works with Transom’s portfolio companies to establish scalable practices and partnerships to design and deliver on business transformation outcomes. He works across many of the portfolio companies to design and execute systems transformations and carve-out transactions.

Prior to joining Transom, John was the Managing Director for a boutique private equity advisory firm. Previously, he spent several decades leading and delivering business transformations for Fortune 100 companies through his tenure at Accenture, Microsoft, and Slalom Consulting.

John received his Bachelor of Business Administration degree in Finance from Pacific Lutheran University.

Quick Fact

John spent 10 years giving back to his community as an elected official as a Council Member and Mayor.
