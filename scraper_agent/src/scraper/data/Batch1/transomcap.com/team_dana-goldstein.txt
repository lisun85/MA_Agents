DANA GOLDSTEIN
Head of Investor Relations & Marketing

Dana Goldstein brings over 15 years of experience in building and leading investor relations and fundraising teams.

She previously led investor relations for Anthos Capital, a growth- and venture-stage investment firm based in Los Angeles. Before this, she spent five years with Levine Leichtman Capital Partners (LLCP), a Los Angeles-based middle-market private equity firm, where she managed fundraising, investor relations, public relations and limited partner co-investment activities. Prior to that, Dana spent seven years with Avenue Capital, based in New York, in various investor relations and business development roles for the firm’s global distressed debt and special situations funds. She previously worked at Morgan Stanley as a member of the Equity Research team and began her career at Bear, Stearns & Co., both in New York.

Dana received a B.S.B.A., magna cum laude, in Finance from Washington University’s Olin School of Business.

Quick Fact

Dana and her family are involved in animal rescue, and often have temporary furry houseguests in addition to their 3 permanent residents.
