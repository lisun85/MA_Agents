TIM OH, CFA
Senior Associate

Tim Oh is a Senior Associate in the M&A team and is responsible for evaluating new investment opportunities, executing acquisitions, and supporting Transom Capital’s existing investment portfolio.

Prior to joining Transom Capital, Tim was an Investment Banking Associate in the Technology Group at Citigroup. Tim also has experiences in working across a range of industries including middle market investment banking, restructuring, and credit investing.

Tim holds a B.S. in Finance and Accounting Info Systems from Boston College. He is also a CFA® charterholder.
