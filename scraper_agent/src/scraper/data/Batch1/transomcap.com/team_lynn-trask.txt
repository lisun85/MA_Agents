LYNN TRASK
Executive Assistant

As Executive Assistant at Transom Capital, Lynn excels in managing intricate schedules, coordinating travel, and overseeing other administrative operations across the firm. Her focus on efficiency and her meticulous attention to detail enable the Transom team to focus on their strategic priorities and achieve peak performance.

Before joining Transom, Lynn served as Executive Assistant to the CEO at ZeroLag Communications, a tech start-up based in Beverly Hills, California. From 2005 until its acquisition in 2017, she also took on in-house Financial Operations, demonstrating her versatility and commitment to organizational excellence.
