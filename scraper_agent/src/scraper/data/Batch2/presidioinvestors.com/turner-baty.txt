TURNER

Vice President

Turner Baty joined Presidio Investors in 2021 as an Associate. Prior to Presidio, Turner was an Associate in the Global Industrials Group at Jefferies LLC, where he advised companies on a variety of capital markets and M&A transactions. He began his career as an Analyst at MIO Partners, Inc. (a subsidiary of McKinsey & Company), a ~$12B AUM asset management firm based in New York City, where he was a member of the Trade Operations team.

He earned his Bachelor of Arts in American Studies and Business from the University of California at Davis.
