TITLE: BluWave Recognizes Heartwood Partners with 2024 Top PE Innovator Award - Heartwood Partners

BASE CONTENT:
================================================================================
Heartwood Partners is honored to be a recipient of the 2024 Top 50 Private Equity Innovator Awards for the third consecutive year. The awards were created by BluWave, a private equity-focused B2B Intelligent Marketplace, to recognize select private equity firms for exemplary innovation and leadership. BluWave’s third annual Top Private Equity Innovator Awards recognize the top 2% of private equity firms that differentially embrace proactive due diligence, transformative value creation, modern private equity firm operations and corporate citizenship practices.
 

“We are honored to be recognized again by BluWave for our commitment to founder, family, and management-held businesses,” said Demetrios Dounis, Partner at Heartwood Partners. “Our people work tirelessly to create an environment where these high quality companies can accomplish their objectives and flourish. This award is a testament to our experience in understanding the needs of entrepreneurial businesses and helping them un-lock their growth potential.”
 
“The 2024 top private equity innovators are providing differential leadership in the world of business,” said Sean Mooney, founder and CEO of BluWave. “Their innovative practices are strengthening companies, creating jobs and catalyzing a rebound in our economy.”
