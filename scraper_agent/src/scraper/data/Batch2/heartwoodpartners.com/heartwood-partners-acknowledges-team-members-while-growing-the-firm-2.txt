In recognition of their strong performance and contributions to the success of our firm, Heartwood Partners is pleased to announce the following promotions:

John Newman, Managing Director
Saer Brown, Vice President
Brian Chung, Vice President
Mike Summe, Senior Associate 
Drew Greene, Senior Associate

Please join us in congratulating each of them for their outstanding contributions to our firm and our investments.

Heartwood Partners Grows its Team
In addition to these promotions, Heartwood Partners is pleased to announce that Tiernan Foster-Smith has joined the firm as an Associate. 

Prior to joining Heartwood Partners, Tiernan was an Associate at Northborne Partners, where he supported the execution of sell-side M&A transactions across the industrial, healthcare and consumer sectors. Previously, he was an Analyst at H2C Securities, a healthcare-focused investment banking and strategic advisory firm.
