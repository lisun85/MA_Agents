We commit to our clients throughout each stage of their development from expansion to maturity.

 

Pleasant Bay Capital Partners is a private equity firm focused on investments in lower-middle market companies.  We partner with management teams and co-investors to maximize the long term value of our portfolio companies. Our opportunistic approach provides us the flexibility to invest across a broad range of opportunities and utilize innovative equity and debt financing structures.  We have investment experience in multiple industries with particular expertise in Healthcare, Technology, and Business Services.
