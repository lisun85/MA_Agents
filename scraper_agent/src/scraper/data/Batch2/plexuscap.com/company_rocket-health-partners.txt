ABOUT

Rocket Health Partners is an integrated dental service organization platform, in partnership with Perio Health Professionals (“PHP”), a leading fee-for-service periodontics practice in the United States. Rocket Health Partners consists of PHP, one orthodontics practice, and four general practices in the Greater Houston Area, with a robust referral pipeline of high-performing dental practices across the country.
