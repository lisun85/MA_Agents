ABOUT

Headquartered in Lexington, NC, Outback Buildings and Bunce Buildings, is a manufacturer and retailer of outdoor storage buildings. The Company operates through a network of 19 dealers and 15 company-owned locations across NC, SC, and VA. Its product line includes sheds, playsets, gazebos, and pole barns made of wood, metal, and vinyl. In addition, Outback & Bunce has a dedicated division for the manufacturing and installation of metal roofing, siding, and carports.
