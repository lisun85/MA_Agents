ABOUT

Headquartered in Aberdeen, MD, Nu-Tek designs and manufactures mission-critical, made-to-specification advanced optics and optomechanical assemblies for a variety of applications, such as high-energy lasers, infrared imaging, spaceflight optics, etc., across defense, space, medical and other scientific end-markets.
