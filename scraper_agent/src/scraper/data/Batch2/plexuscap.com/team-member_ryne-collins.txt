Ryne Collins
VICE PRESIDENT
BIOGRAPHY

Ryne joined Plexus in 2020 as an Associate and is now a Vice President, focusing on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring. He began his career as a U.S. Air Force Officer, serving as an F-15E Weapon Systems Officer, spanning 11 years of service. After leaving Active Duty, Ryne worked as an Associate at UBS in the Financial Institutions Group, providing investment banking and capital markets solutions to clients in the insurance industry.

Ryne is a graduate of The Citadel, where he earned his B.S. in Electrical Engineering. Ryne received an MBA from Duke University, and an M.A. focused in International Relations from the University of Oklahoma.
