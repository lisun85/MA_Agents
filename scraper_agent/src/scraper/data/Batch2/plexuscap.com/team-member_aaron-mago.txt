BIOGRAPHY

Aaron joined Plexus in 2024 and is an Associate on the investment team. Aaron focuses on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring.

Prior to joining Plexus, Aaron worked as an Investment Banking Analyst at Truist Securities where he focused on providing M&A advisory services to clients in the Telecom industry.

Aaron is a graduate of the University of Florida where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Finance and a Master of Science in Finance.
