Ian Wooley
VICE PRESIDENT
BIOGRAPHY

Ian joined Plexus in 2021 and is a Vice President on the investment team and focuses on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring.

Prior to joining Plexus, Ian worked on the investment team at Stellex Capital Management as well as in the Investment Bank at Bank of America Merrill Lynch.

Ian is a graduate of Yale University where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics.
