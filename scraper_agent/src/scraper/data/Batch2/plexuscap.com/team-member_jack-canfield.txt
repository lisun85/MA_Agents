Jack Canfield
SENIOR ASSOCIATE
BIOGRAPHY

Jack joined Plexus in 2022 and is a Senior Associate focusing on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring. Prior to joining Plexus, Jack began his career as an Associate at CVF Capital Partners, a lower middle market private equity firm in Sacramento, where he worked on deal origination, execution, and portfolio company management.

Jack is a graduate of Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo where he earned a Bachelor of Science, summa cum laude, in Business Administration with a concentration in finance and a minor in Spanish. Jack is also a CFA charterholder.
