Louis Robinson
ASSOCIATE
BIOGRAPHY

Louis joined Plexus in 2024 and is an Associate on the investment team. Louis focuses on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring.

Prior to joining Plexus, Louis worked as an Investment Banking Analyst at Deloitte Corporate Finance where he provided M&A advisory services to clients in the Consumer and Retail industry.

Louis is a graduate of Michigan State University where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in Finance.
