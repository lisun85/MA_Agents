Barrett Biringer
PRINCIPAL
BIOGRAPHY

Barrett joined Plexus in 2014. He is a Principal and focuses on deal execution, portfolio monitoring, and business development.

Barrett began his career at Stephens, Inc., a middle market investment banking firm, then joined SunTrust Robinson Humphrey (STRH) in Atlanta. At STRH, he focused on credit analysis, deal execution, and portfolio monitoring.

Barrett is a graduate of the University of North Carolina where he earned an MBA and of the University of Georgia where he earned a Bachelor of Business Administration.
