ABOUT

Headquartered in Midlothian, TX, Commercial Truck and Trailer Parts (CTTP) and M&R Manufacturing and Supply is a national distributor of aftermarket parts for the U.S. heavy-duty truck and trailer industry and manufacturer of intermodal chassis.
