BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE ANALYST
BIOGRAPHY

Andrew joined Plexus in 2022 and is a Business Intelligence Analyst. Before joining Plexus, Andrew worked for Truist supporting their Audit Innovation and Analytics group as a Data Scientist. There he supported audit teams with a focus on data project management, audit engagement analytics support, and automation. He joined Plexus to support the growth of their data and analytics capabilities.

Andrew graduated from North Carolina State University with a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering in 2019 and a Master of Science in Analytics in 2021.
