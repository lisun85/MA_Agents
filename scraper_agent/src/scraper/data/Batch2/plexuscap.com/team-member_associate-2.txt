BIOGRAPHY

Robbie joined Plexus in 2023 as an Associate and focuses on fund management, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence and portfolio monitoring. Robbie began his career as an Analyst and then Associate at Truist Securities, where he provided Investment Banking services in the Consumer & Retail industry.

Robbie is a graduate of the University of North Carolina where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and was a member of the Varsity Men’s Basketball team.
