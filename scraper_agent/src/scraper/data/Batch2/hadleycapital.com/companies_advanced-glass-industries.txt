Learn About The Company

Advanced Glass Industries is one of the world's leading manufacturers of precision machined optical glass blanks. Founded more than 75 years ago and based in Rochester, NY, AGI cores, cuts, machines, and polishes highly technical bulk glass material sourced from the world’s leading foundries to meet its customers unique specifications. AGI’s products are found in diverse end industries requiring precision optics including aviation, transportation, defense, medical, satellite, and others.

The Situation

As an owner-managed company, the two aging partners – both of whom were actively involved on a day-to-day basis - needed a transition solution that provided both a road map toward retirement and liquidity to secure their financial future.
