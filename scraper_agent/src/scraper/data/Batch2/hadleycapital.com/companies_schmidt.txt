Learn About The Company

Schmidt designs, builds and implements marking systems for manufacturing companies throughout North America. The company is at the forefront of innovative marking technology and has a comprehensive range of marking systems that provide permanent identification and traceability for any part. Schmidt is based in Niles, Illinois

The Situation

We partnered with Schmidt’s key employees to purchase the company from absentee owners who were seeking liquidity.

The Outcome

Schmidt recently celebrated its 125th birthday. Under our ownership, Schmidt has completed an acquisition and introduced new products.
