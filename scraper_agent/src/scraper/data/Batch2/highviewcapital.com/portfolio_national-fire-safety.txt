NATIONAL FIRE & SAFETY

National Fire & Safety is a leading end-to-end fire and life safety solutions platform with operations in seven states across the Western U.S. The company specializes in fire sprinkler system design, installation, inspections, and repair services.

The company leverages the local knowledge and expertise of the nation’s most trusted brands through its independent subsidiaries Frontier Fire Protection, Elite FPS, and RCI Systems. Collectively the company delivers a comprehensive suite of fire sprinkler and alarm services to a variety of end markets, including commercial, industrial, institutional, and residential. National Fire & Safety installs over 2 million linear feet of steel pipe for its customers annually.
