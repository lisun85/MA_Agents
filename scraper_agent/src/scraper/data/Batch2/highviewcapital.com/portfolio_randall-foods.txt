RANDALL FOODS

Randall Foods is a diversified protein supplier of scale that serves a blue-chip customer base across the grocery and foodservice markets. The company offers a broad array of SKUs across poultry, beef, ground beef, and pork, as well as value-added products including marinated and ready-to-cook offerings that present a one-stop shop for its customers’ protein needs.

Randall differentiates itself through its scale, product assortment, and service encompassing every aspect of the customer experience. The company has an unmatched commitment to its core base and continues to cultivate new ones while focusing on quality, agility, and innovation. Randall’s ability to execute orders on short lead times have made the company a distinct, value-add partner to its customers.
