Charlie Alutto

Operating Advisor

PROFESSIONAL

Charlie has had a 30+ year career in healthcare, business to business, industrial and logistic service sectors. He joined the Board of Safety Marking, a Highview portfolio company, in 2025 and serves as an independent director on various public and private company boards.

Previously, Charlie was the CEO and President of Stericycle, Inc. (NASDAQ: SRCL) which at the time was a $3.6B global company and was the largest medical waste and document destruction company in the United States and around the world. Earlier in his career Charlie had various leadership positions at Stericycle and began his career at Environmental Control Co, Inc., a regional waste and compliance company.

EDUCATION

Charlie earned his B.S. in Finance from Providence College, RI and an MBA from St. John’s University, NY.
