Christian Boorom

Senior Associate

PROFESSIONAL

Christian Boorom is a Senior Associate at Highview Capital. Christian is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence, and execution of new investments.

Prior to joining Highview, Christian began his career at Deloitte Consulting before working with L.E.K. Consulting, where he focused on mergers and acquisitions across a variety of sectors, including consumer, industrials, and healthcare.

EDUCATION

Christian holds a B.B.A. in Accounting from Southern Methodist University and was a four-year letterman on the varsity soccer team.

PERSONAL

Christian grew up in Fort Lauderdale, FL and now resides in Santa Monica. In his free time, he enjoys anything competitive as well as skiing, hiking, and endurance sports. He also doesn’t miss a football season and has been known as a key lime pie connoisseur.
