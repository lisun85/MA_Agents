Dan Picmann

Managing Director

PROFESSIONAL

Dan Picmann is a Managing Director at Highview Capital. Dan is a founding member of the Highview team and is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence and execution of new investments. In addition, Dan leads all debt capital placement and related corporate finance activity for new and existing portfolio companies. Dan currently serves on the Board of National Fire & Safety, Quickplay and Randall Foods.

Prior to joining Highview, Dan worked as a Senior Associate at Post Advisory Group, a Los Angeles-based credit hedge fund, where he executed high-yield bond and leveraged loan investments in the technology sector.

Earlier in his career, Dan worked in the Leveraged Finance Group at J.P. Morgan and at Bank of America’s Special Assets Group, where he restructured distressed middle-market credits during the Great Recession.

EDUCATION

Dan holds a B.B.A. in Finance and Accounting from Western Michigan University and an M.B.A. from the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan. During his undergraduate years, he was also a member of Western Michigan’s varsity soccer team.

PERSONAL

Dan grew up in Michigan and lives in Los Angeles with his wife and two sons. He is a die-hard University of Michigan and Detroit sports fan (he knows they can hear him – they need him). In his free time, Dan enjoys golf, tennis, soccer, cooking and traveling with his family. Dan’s birthday is on Groundhog’s Day, so you can always check with him if spring will come early.
