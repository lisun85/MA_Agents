Alexander Ott

Vice President

PROFESSIONAL

Alex Ott is a Vice President at Highview Capital. Alex is responsible for designing and leading value creation programs across the portfolio.

Prior to joining Highview, Alex was a Vice President with Sun Capital Partners, where he focused on value creation initiatives across platforms in a variety of sectors, including healthcare, business services, consumer products, and shipping & logistics.

EDUCATION

Alex holds a M.B.A. from the University of Virginia Darden School of Business and a B.S.B.A in International Business from the University of South Carolina.

PERSONAL

Alex grew up outside of Washington, D.C., and now resides in Seattle, WA with his wife and daughter (with stops in New Orleans, Austin, and New York along the way). In his free time, he enjoys getting lost in the mountains and seeing how high he can push his golf score.
