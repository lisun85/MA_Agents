Kenneth Slutsky

Senior Advisor

PROFESSIONAL

Kenneth Slutsky is an investor in both private and public sectors enterprises. His experience spans numerous industries including: technology, energy, real estate and financial services. Among the corporations in which he has held CEO or other senior leadership positions are Kern Oil & Refining Co, Casey Co, and Candle Corporation, a leading provider of systems management software.

He has served or is serving on an advisory board for Northwest Airlines, Red Cloud Capital, AT Curd/PCL Construction Co, Wedgewood Enterprise Corporation, and the Middle East Policy Board for The Rand Corp, among others. Kenny also has been involved with several public sector endeavors including, but not limited to, running political campaigns and providing advice to various governmental agencies.

EDUCATION

Kenny graduated magna cum laude from Bowdoin College and obtained a J.D. from Emory University School of Law.
