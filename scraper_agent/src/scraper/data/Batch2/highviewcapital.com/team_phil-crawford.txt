Phil Crawford

Vice President

PROFESSIONAL

Phil Crawford is a Vice President at Highview Capital. Phil is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence, and execution of new investments Phil began his career at Morgan Stanley where he worked in Equity Derivative Sales & Trading and Equity Research as part of a rotational leadership program before transitioning to Industrials Investment Banking. Phil currently serves on the Board of Quickplay and National Fire & Safety.

EDUCATION

Phil holds a M.A. in Finance and a B.A. in Economics from Claremont McKenna College where he graduated as a Robert Day Scholar. While at Claremont, Phil played football and lacrosse and served as President of the college’s Student Investment Fund.

PERSONAL

Phil grew up in the northern suburbs of Chicago and now lives in Venice. He enjoys all outdoor activities including tennis, paddle boarding, fishing, and hiking. When indoors, Phil reads Steinbeck, doesn’t miss a Chicago Bears game, and tends to be overly competitive at backgammon.
