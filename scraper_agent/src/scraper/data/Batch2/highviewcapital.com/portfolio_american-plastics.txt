American Plastics

AMERICAN PLASTICS

American Plastics is the 2nd largest designer, manufacturer, and distributor of innovative, environmentally-friendly home storage, home organization, and commercial cleaning products in the United States.

The company utilizes primarily recycled inputs and maintains a national manufacturing and distribution footprint with 13 state-of-the-art facilities across North America, bringing American-made quality to blue-chip retailers and distributors nationwide. American Plastics reuses recycled material equivalent to 8 billion water bottles on an annual basis.
