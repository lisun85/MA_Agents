P.J. Gilbert

Managing Director

PROFESSIONAL

P.J. Gilbert is a Managing Director at Highview Capital. P.J. is a founding member of the Highview team and is responsible for all aspects of the investment process including sourcing, diligence and execution of new investments. P.J. currently serves on the Board of B&R Auto, Five Star Energy Services, National Fire & Safety, Safety Marking and Watterson. P.J. previously served on the Board of American Plastics.

Prior to joining Highview, P.J. worked as a Vice President at JLL Partners, a New York-based private equity fund, where he executed investments across industrials, business services, healthcare and other sectors.

Earlier in his career, P.J. was an Associate at Lightyear Capital and an investment banking Analyst at Bank of America.

EDUCATION

P.J. holds a B.S. in Economics and an M.B.A. with Honors from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. During his undergraduate years, he was a member of the University of Pennsylvania’s varsity lacrosse team.

PERSONAL

P.J. grew up outside Baltimore, MD and now lives in Santa Monica with his wife, daughter, and mini goldendoodle. He is especially passionate about private equity’s role in family businesses having grown-up in a 4th-generation family produce company. P.J. serves on the Board of Directors of non-profit organization Friends of Golf and in his free time, he enjoys golfing, skateboarding, and considers himself a “big appetizers guy."
