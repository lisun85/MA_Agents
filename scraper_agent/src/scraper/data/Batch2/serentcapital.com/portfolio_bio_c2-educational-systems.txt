C2 EDUCATIONAL SYSTEMS

http://www.c2educate.com/

Atlanta, GA

C2 Educational Systems is a leading provider of test preparation and academic tutoring services to K-12 students. With help from C2’s rigorous curriculum and innovative delivery model, 85% of C2’s US students are admitted to a Top 50 university. C2 currently has over 100 locations and is growing rapidly.
