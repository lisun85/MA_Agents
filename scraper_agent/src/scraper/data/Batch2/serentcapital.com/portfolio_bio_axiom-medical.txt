AXIOM MEDICAL

http://www.axiomllc.com/

The Woodlands, TX

Axiom Medical is a market leading full-service provider of occupational health services that support the employee journey from pre-hire to post-separation. Their occupational health programs help clients ensure a healthy workforce that is capable of performing all tasks expected of each role. Their 24/7 incident and injury case management services manage optimal outcomes for employee and employer when a workplace injury occurs, and their absence management service helps employees, who are out on extended absence due to personal health matters, return to the workplace more quickly than traditional approaches.
