Alex Stanek
Manager

Alex is a manager at Kainos focused on strengthening the operational performance of the firm’s partner companies’ supply chains and overseeing strategic purchasing initiatives across the portfolio. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos, she began her consulting career at USC Consulting Group as an associate consultant and subsequently as a project manager, where she managed internal and client project teams’ supply chain and spend management practice. Alex has a B.S. in Civil Engineering from University of Missouri.

 

Alex is highly involved in the University of Missouri and Gamma Phi Beta alumni groups. She volunteers at the humane society and supports the Anti-Cruelty Society, among other charities she supports.
