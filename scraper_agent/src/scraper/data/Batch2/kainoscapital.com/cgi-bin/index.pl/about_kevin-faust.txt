Kevin Faust
Fund Controller

Kevin Faust is a fund controller and brings over 14 years of accounting experience to Kainos. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2016, he worked as an accounting director at Peloton Commercial Real Estate for two years, vice president at Stream Realty Partners for nine years, and as a senior accountant at KPMG in their tax group for three years.

 

Kevin received his B.S. and M.S. in Accounting from the University of North Texas in 2002.
