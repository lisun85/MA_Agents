Catherine Anne Prideaux
Vice President

Catherine Anne is a vice president at Kainos with a focus on evaluating, executing, and managing investment opportunities in the food and consumer products sectors.  

 

Catherine Anne first joined Kainos in 2018 as an associate and rejoined in 2023. Prior to joining Kainos, she began her career as an investment banker with Morgan Stanley’s Natural Resources Group in Houston.

 

She holds an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and graduated with the highest distinction from The University of Texas at Austin with a B.A. in Plan II Honors and a B.B.A in the Business Honors Program.
