Jeff Moredock
Partner

Jeff is a Partner at Kainos leading the evaluation, execution, and management of investment opportunities in the food, pet, specialty distribution, ingredients, and animal health industries. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2017, he was a vice president at CenterOak Partners and an associate at Brazos Partners. Jeff began his career as an investment banker with Deutsche Bank’s M&A Group in New York.

 

He holds an MBA from Harvard Business School where he graduated with distinction, and a B.A. in Economics and History, magna cum laude with honors, from Vanderbilt University. He serves on the board of the Meadowbrook School and volunteers at his church.
