Jacob Askew
Associate

Jacob Askew is an associate at Kainos with a focus on evaluating, executing, and monitoring investment opportunities. He enjoys working with portfolio management teams to execute on operational and strategic growth initiatives that positively transform their businesses.

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2024, he was employed as an Investment Banking Analyst at Goldman Sachs in New York.

 

Jacob graduated with High Honors from the University of Texas at Austin with a B.B.A. in Business Honors and Finance. Jacob supports Texas Young Life and Wall Street for McCombs.
