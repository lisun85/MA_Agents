David Gassko
Partner

David Gassko is a Partner at Kainos Capital. David first joined Kainos in 2013 as an associate and rejoined the firm in 2018 as Vice President. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos, David began his career as an investment banker with Wells Fargo Securities.

 

He holds an MBA from The Wharton School where he graduated with honors and a B.A. in Economics and English, cum laude with honors, from Rice University. David is highly involved with his alma mater through the Rice Volunteers for Admission, Rice Annual Fund, and Rice DFW Alumni organizations.
