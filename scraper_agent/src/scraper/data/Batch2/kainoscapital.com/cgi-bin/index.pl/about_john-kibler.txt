John Kibler
Senior Associate

John is an associate at Kainos Capital. 

 

Prior to joining Kainos in 2022, John was an analyst in the Global Energy Group at Morgan Stanley, where he focused on M&A advisory and capital markets financing.

 

John graduated cum laude and with business honors from Southern Methodist University with a B.B.A. in Finance, a specialization in Alternative Asset Management, and a B.A. in Economics.
