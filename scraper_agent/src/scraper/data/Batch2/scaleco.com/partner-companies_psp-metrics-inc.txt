Overview

Founded in 1946 by the famed Dr. Frederick Herzberg and based in Pittsburgh, PA, PSP Metrics, Inc. (“PSP” or the “Company”) is a global leader in research-based employee testing.

PSP provides pre-employment and post-employment assessment testing solutions and related consulting services covering talent assessment and development and organizational design.

The Company’s software platform, which includes over 40 proprietary assessments, serves all levels of employment within an organization evaluating candidates in problem-solving, communication, teamwork, adaptability, motivation, and learning capacity, among others.

With more than 75 years of experience, PSP partners with blue-chip companies across the utility, business services, homebuilding, and manufacturing industries to help them maximize workforce performance, reduce turnover, and improve employee satisfaction.
