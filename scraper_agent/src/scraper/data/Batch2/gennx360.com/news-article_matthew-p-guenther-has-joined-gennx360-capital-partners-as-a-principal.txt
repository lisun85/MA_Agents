Matthew P. Guenther has Joined GenNx360 Capital Partners as a Principal
Portfolio Company News - January 03, 2007

Matthew joined GenNx360 Capital Partners as a Principal following 8 years at Walden Capital Partners LP.

At Walden Capital, Matthew was involved in both new investment and portfolio management. He has experience in sourcing, evaluating, structuring, and negotiating investment opportunities, as well as monitoring and working with existing portfolio companies. Matthew worked with companies in a wide range of industries resulting in the development of a diversified and well-rounded business background.

Prior to Walden, Matthew was at KPMG Consulting in their strategic/financial planning and analysis department. While at KPMG, he was responsible for developing business plans, analyzing acquisition targets and managing strategic initiatives for the consulting practice. Matthew began his career at AT&T where he worked in various sales, marketing and finance positions.

Matthew earned a B.A. in History from Union College and an M.B.A. in Finance and International Business from Columbia Business School.
