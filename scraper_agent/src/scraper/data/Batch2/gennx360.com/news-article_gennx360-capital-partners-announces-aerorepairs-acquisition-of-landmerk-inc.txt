GenNx360 Capital Partners Announces AeroRepair’s Acquisition of Landmerk Inc.
Portfolio Company News - October 15, 2021


New York, New York.  October 15, 2021 – GenNx360 Capital Partners (“GenNx360”) is pleased to announce its portfolio company AeroRepair Corp (“AeroRepair”), has acquired Landmerk Inc (“Landmerk”) located in South Miami, Florida.  The acquisition represents AeroRepair’s second add-on acquisition since GenNx360’s investment in the platform in December 2019. 
 
Landmerk is an FAA (EASA) certified Aircraft Wheel and Brake Repair Station. Established in 2001, Landmerk offers NDT services and overhaul and repairs for all types of wheels and brakes, supplying tires from different manufactures, including Bridgestone, Michelin, Goodyear and Dunlop. They also provide 24/7 AOG services.
 
 
About AeroRepair Corp
AeroRepair Corp is the largest independent Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (“MRO”) platform in North America with a focus on wheels, brakes, batteries and landing gear, serving a diverse group of regional airlines as well as corporate fleets and narrow and wide body aircraft. AeroRepair offers its customers complete wheel and brake management solutions including high-quality workmanship, leading turn-times, asset management and logistics services. Founded in 1994, and now part of the GenNx360 Portfolio of Companies, AeroRepair is headquartered in Manchester, New Hampshire and has additional facilities in Atlanta, Georgia, Indianapolis, Indiana, Phoenix, Arizona, Montreal Quebec and Calgary, Alberta.
For more information, please visit www.aerorepaircorp.com.
 
About GenNx360 Capital Partners
GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity firm focused on investing in middle market business services and industrial companies.  GenNx360 partners with companies having proven and sustainable business models in expanding industries, with the objective of implementing and supporting value enhancing organic and inorganic initiatives to accelerate growth, deliver efficiencies and generate strong financial returns. GenNx360 was founded in 2006 and is headquartered in New York City. For more information, please visit www.gennx360.com.
