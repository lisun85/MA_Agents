GenNx360 Capital Partners Announces Miller Environmental Group’s Acquisition of Monarch Environmental Services, Inc.
Portfolio Company News - November 30, 2020


New York, New York.  November 30, 2020 - GenNx360 Capital Partners (“GenNx360”), a New York-based private equity firm, is pleased to announce that its portfolio company, Miller Environmental Group Inc. (“MEG”), a leading provider of environmental and emergency response services in the Eastern United States, announced that it has acquired Monarch Environmental Services, Inc (“Monarch”). Monarch is Miller Environmental Group’s second acquisition since GenNx360’s investment in the platform in March 2019. 
 
Headquartered in Mannington, New Jersey, Monarch is a leader in waste and disposal services and has been serving clients along the East Coast since 1989. Monarch provides environmental response, remediation, transportation and recycling services at its two Treatment Storage and Disposal Facilities (TSDF) located in Woodstown, New Jersey and Tampa, Florida.
The combined company will have a footprint of 28 locations across the Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, and Southeast regions in addition to the ability to respond to emergencies on a national basis through its managed network of third-party emergency response vendors.
MEG President and CEO, Jerry Coogan, said, “In keeping with our commitment to serving our clients with safety and efficiency, we are thrilled to add this vertical integration to our company, allowing MEG to solve clients’ problems with a one-stop solution.” Coogan added, “This acquisition adds a total of 3 locations to our footprint with the addition of an Operations Center in Mannington, New Jersey, and two Treatment, Storage and Disposal Facilities (TSDFs) and Operation Centers.”
About Miller
MEG, founded in 1971, is an industry-leading provider of emergency response, environmental remediation, and other industrial services for clients in various industries including marine, rail, oil & gas, transportation, power & utility, and manufacturing. MEG provides services on both an emergency response and project basis while maintaining an exemplary record for safety. MEG holds the highest Oil Spill Removal Organization (OSRO) classification offered in its area of service. MEG currently operates in 10 locations in the Northeast and in the Mid-Atlantic United States and counts several Fortune 500 companies amongst its varied client base. www.millerenv.com
