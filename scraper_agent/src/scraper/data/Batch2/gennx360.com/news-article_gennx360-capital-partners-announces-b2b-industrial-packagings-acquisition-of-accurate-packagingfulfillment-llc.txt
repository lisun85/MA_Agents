GenNx360 Capital Partners Announces B2B Industrial Packaging’s Acquisition of Accurate Packaging & Fulfillment, LLC
Portfolio Company News - January 05, 2022


New York, New York.  January 5, 2022 – GenNx360 Capital Partners (“GenNx360”) is pleased to announce its portfolio company, B2B Industrial Packaging (“B2B”), has acquired Accurate Packaging & Fulfillment, LLC (“Accurate”) in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Accurate is B2B Industrial Packaging’s eighth acquisition since GenNx360’s investment in the platform in October 2020.
 
Accurate Packaging & Fulfillment, LLC offers contract packaging, fulfillment, trucking, shrink films, tapes and boxes among other supplies.  In addition to production areas and warehouse storage, their facility offers a conditioned state of the art clean room.  All space is access controlled for safe storage of product. Customers are a top priority, with a pledge to save customers money by offering the most up-to-date systems to increase production rates and minimize waste.
 
This acquisition will deliver many benefits to Accurate clients who will enjoy the big company resources that B2B Industrial Packaging offers such as more products and services, a large portfolio of channel partners, and a deep bench of expertise; along with the individual attention that only a smaller company typically provides.
 
About B2B Industrial Packaging
Servicing more than 6,000 active clients, B2B Industrial Packaging sells a full range of packaging equipment and supplies including steel strapping, stretch film, and fasteners to clients throughout the U.S. and Mexico. B2B Industrial Packaging is unique in that it also operates three state-of-the-art strapping and fastener tool repair facilities that service the entire U.S. Headquarters are in Addison, Illinois with additional locations in Phoenix, Arizona; Houston and Fort Worth, Texas; Seattle, Washington; Los Angeles, Salinas, Hayward and Sacramento, California; Portland, Eugene, and Albany, Oregon; Kansas City and Shawnee, Kansas; Kalamazoo, Michigan;  Harrisburg, Pennsylvania and Oregon, Illinois.
 
About GenNx360 Capital Partners
GenNx360 Capital Partners is a private equity firm focused on investing in middle market business services and industrial companies.  GenNx360 partners with companies having proven and sustainable business models in expanding industries, with the objective of implementing and supporting value enhancing organic and inorganic initiatives to accelerate growth, deliver efficiencies and generate strong financial returns. GenNx360 was founded in 2006 and is headquartered in New York City. For more information, please visit www.gennx360.com.
