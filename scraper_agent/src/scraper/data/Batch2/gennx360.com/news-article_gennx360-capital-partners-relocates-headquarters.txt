GenNx360 Capital Partners Relocates Headquarters
Portfolio Company News - August 17, 2009

GenNx360 Capital Partners, a private equity firm focused on industrial business-to-business companies with $100 million to $1 billion in revenues, announced today the firm has relocated its midtown Manhattan headquarters from a temporary to permanent location. The firm is now located in the former IBM building on the southwest corner of Madison Avenue and 57th Street. Our New York office address is now:

GenNx360 Capital Partners 590 Madison Avenue 27th Floor New York, NY 10022

The company's telephone number remains 888-215-6992.

GenNx360 also has offices in Seattle, WA, Cambridge, MA, and Gurgaon and Bangalore, India.
