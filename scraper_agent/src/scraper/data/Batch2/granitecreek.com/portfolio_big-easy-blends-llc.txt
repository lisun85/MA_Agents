Big Easy Blends, LLC
NEW ORLEANS, LA

Big Easy Blends is a manufacturer and developer of pre-mixed frozen alcoholic and nonalcoholic beverages in flexible, portable pouches. The products are sold in grocery, convenience, and discount stores around the country. The company was founded in 2007 by Sal LaMartina, Craig Cordes and Antonio LaMartina. The company is headquartered in New Orleans, LA.
