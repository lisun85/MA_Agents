Odyssey Aviation U.S.

Odyssey is a fixed-base operator (‘FBO’) that provides aviation fuel, hangar rental, aircraft de-icing, and other services to private aviation customers and air cargo operators at the Kissimmee Gateway Airport in Kissimmee, FL and the Willow Run Airport in Ypsilanti, MI.

In early 2023, Odyssey acquired Golden Isles Aviation, an FBO serving McKinnon St. Simons Island Airport (“SSI”) on St. Simons Island, GA, a resort destination on Georgia’s Atlantic coast.
