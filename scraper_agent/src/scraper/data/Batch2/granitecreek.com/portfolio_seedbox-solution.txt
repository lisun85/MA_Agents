Seedbox Solution
GURNEE, ILLINOIS

Seedbox is a full-service provider of reusable packaging and fleet management solutions to the row crop seed industry with a focus on plastic seed boxes. With over 75 years of combined expertise in reusable packaging systems, Seedbox is focused on providing products and services that make using bulk plastic seed boxes simpler, easier, and more affordable.
