Ashland Foundry & Machine Works, Inc. (Ashalnd) manufactures complex, highly engineered steel, chrome iron, and high-alloy castings, ranging in size from 50 to 7,500 pounds, primarily for OEMs of pumps. The Ashland facility has operated continuously since 1881 and strives to provide high-quality products and services to customers. The company has an AOD, extensive machining facility, simulation software, and in-house 3-D printing to provide customers with a range of technical capabilities and the ability to cast, machine, assemble, and test large complex products.

Ashalnd’s sister company, West Coast Stainless Products, manufactures impellers for OEMs of pumps in various high-alloy steels, Ni-Resist (a hard, temperature-resistant grade of iron), and other exotics. Dynatek, Inc., a sister company to West Coast, provides machining services that include turning, boring, polishing, and pressure testing.
