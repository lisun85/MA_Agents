Golden West Fruit Company, Inc. (“Golden West”) owns and operates fruit pasteurization and processing plants and manufactures fruit fillings and fruit flavor products for use in bakery and pie fillings, yogurt fruit, and specialty restaurant desserts. Founded in 1928, the company is based in Commerce, California. Sweet Ovations completed its bolt-on acquisition of Golden West in May 2012.
