Stahl Specialty Company, a carve-out from ThyssenKrupp, is a dominant supplier of machined permanent mold aluminum castings serving the heavy truck, marine, heavy industry, and automotive end markets. Speyside Equity exited Stahl Specialty Company in 2010. The transaction completed a successful investment during the financial crisis and included a period of restructuring and refocusing the company for future growth.
