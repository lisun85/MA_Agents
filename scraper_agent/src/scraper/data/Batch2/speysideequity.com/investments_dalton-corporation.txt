Since 1914, Dalton Corporation in Warsaw, Indiana, has been a leading manufacturer of large, highly-cored, and complex iron castings for agriculture, railroad, construction, air conditioning/refrigeration, heads/blocks, power transmissions, material handling, and medium/heavy-duty truck transmissions. The company delivers exceptionally consistent quality, easy machinability—on time. Dalton completed its acquisition of Motor Castings Corporation in October 2017.
