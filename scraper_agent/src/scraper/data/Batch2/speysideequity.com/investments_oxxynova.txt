Dating back to 1958 and based in Steyerberg, Germany, Oxxynova is a mid-sized chemical company that specializes in the production and marketing of liquid dimethyl terephthalate (“DMT”). As a leading manufacturer in Europe, Oxxynova continues to innovate DMT and related xylol-derivative products. DMT is used in many everyday products, including seat belts, food packaging, tarps, and medical devices. Recently, Oxxynova has developed a second business line geared toward chemical re-purification.
