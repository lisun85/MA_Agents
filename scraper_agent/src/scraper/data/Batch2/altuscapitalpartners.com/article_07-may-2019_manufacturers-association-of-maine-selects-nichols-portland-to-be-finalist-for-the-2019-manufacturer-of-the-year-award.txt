Manufacturers Association of Maine selects Nichols Portland to be finalist for the 2019 Manufacturer of the Year Award

             May 2, 2019

 

Thomas Houck, President & CEO Nichols Portland                              

2400 Congress Street

Portland, Maine 04102

 

Dear Thomas,

We are pleased to inform you that Nichols Portland has been selected as one of three finalists for the 2019 "Manufacturer of the Year" award!

This prestigious award, being given at the 2019 Manufacturing Summit, recognizes manufacturers that are outstanding in the areas of supporting employees, business and community as well as exceptional business practices!

Please join us on May 17, 2019, from 7:30AM-1PM at Lost Valley Ski Area where we will announce the winner!

As our guest, please register up to five Nichols Portland employees using the promo code:PREPAID19 to get your guest passes. Please RSVP by May 13.

We look forward to seeing you and wish you all the luck!

Sincerely, 

 

Lisa G. Martin

Executive Director
