Altus Capital Co-Founder Says Coronavirus Could Drive More Manufacturing Back to U.S. ~ The manufacturing-focused private-equity firm sees the virus outbreak fueling a move to repatriate production operations from offshore sites

March 13, 2020

 
With an increasing number of companies relocated manufacturing to U.S. plants over the past few years, Russ Greenberg of Altus Capital Partners was featured in an industry article in WSJ Pro on the topic of reshoring and believes the trend likely will accelerate, as the coronavirus outbreak underlines the risks of relying on products made in far-off locations. Altus Capital has deep experience in the U.S. manufacturing space, investing in middle market manufacturing businesses has been their focus since 2003.
