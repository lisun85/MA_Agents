Thomas R. Groh

Partner

Tom joined Altus in 2014. Prior to joining the Firm, he was a Director at KPMG Corporate Finance, the middle market investment banking subsidiary of KPMG LLP, where he focused on M&A and strategic advisory engagements.

Prior to KPMG, he worked in investment banking at NatCity Investments, Inc. and in commercial banking at National City Bank (subsidiaries of National City Corporation which was acquired by PNC Financial Services Group, Inc. in 2008). He has worked with middle market manufacturing companies throughout his career.

Tom earned an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business, an MPA from the Indiana University Kelley School of Business, and a BBA from the Western Michigan University Haworth College of Business.

Tom serves on the Board of Directors of Thermal Solutions Manufacturing, Inc.
