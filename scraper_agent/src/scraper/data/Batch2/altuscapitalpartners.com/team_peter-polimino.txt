Peter Polimino

Partner, Chief Financial Officer,
Chief Compliance Officer

Mr. Polimino joined Altus Capital Partners in 2008 and is responsible for the firm’s back-office operations, compliance program, financial operations and investor relations.

Peter Polimino, has been in the private equity business since 1998 and has over 40 years of experience in finance, operations and business management. 

Peter started his career at Westinghouse Electric, rising through several managerial positions at Group W Broadcasting and CBS Inc. Later he served as Vice President, Sales Finance at Fox Sports Net before moving onto private equity. He has worked on due diligence teams on numerous acquisitions, divestitures, start-ups, and mergers in the media sector.

Peter is a graduate of Iona College, where he earned a BBA in Accounting and an MBA in Production and Operations Management.

Peter serves on the Board of Directors of Thomson Plastics, Inc.
