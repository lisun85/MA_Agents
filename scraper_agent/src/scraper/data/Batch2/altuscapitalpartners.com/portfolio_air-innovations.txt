Air Innovations is a designer and builder of environmental process control systems for applications that cannot be addressed with standard HVAC equipment. The company customizes packaged solutions for temperature, humidity, filtration, and pressurization. Originally founded as Floratech in 1986, Air Innovations has been committed to ‘enabling a higher quality of life’ for nearly 40 years.
