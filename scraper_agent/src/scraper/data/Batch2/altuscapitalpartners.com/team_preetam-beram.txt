Preetam Beram

Associate

Preetam joined Altus Capital Partners in January 2021. Preetam began his career as an analyst at RHK Capital, within the equity capital markets division.

He brings to the Altus Team his research and strong financial analysis skills. His responsibilities include conducting due diligence on prospective investments, performing financial modelling along with monitoring the portfolio companies and business development initiatives.

Preetam received a B.S. in Finance from the University of Connecticut.
