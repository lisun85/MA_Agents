Andrew Matney

Vice President

Andrew joined Altus Capital Partners in 2023. Prior to joining the Firm, he was an Investment Banking Associate at Lincoln International focused on sell-side M&A transactions. Prior to that, he was at Park Sutton Advisors in a similar role. His previous engagement was at Wells Fargo where he was an Assistant Vice President working on debt offerings, credit operations, and client relationships. Andrew started his career at Capital Bank in loan origination.

Andrew earned an MBA from the Indiana University Kelley School of Business and a BBA from the University of Texas at El Paso. He holds licenses in FINRA Series 79 and NASAA Series 63.
