ERIC KORSTEN
Position: Senior Managing DirectorCategories: sidebar, team

Mr. Korsten is a Senior Managing Director and Investment Committee member of Branford. He has been active in private equity and finance for over 25 years. Mr. Korsten is currently a board member of ABC Industries, Eastern Dental Management, Hoffman Engineering and Testek Solutions. Prior to re-joining Branford, he held senior roles in private equity. Before then, Mr. Korsten was a Senior Managing Director and Investment Committee member at Branford, where he worked for over a decade. While at Branford, he led numerous investments across a variety of industries and held board seats on a number of boards, including notable investments in Surface Preparation Technologies and Pulse Veterinary Technologies. Early in his career, Mr. Korsten was an investment banker focused on M&A, restructuring and capital markets at several investment banks, including Jefferies & Co.

Mr. Korsten graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a B.A. and holds an M.B.A. from New York University – Leonard N. Stern School of Business.
