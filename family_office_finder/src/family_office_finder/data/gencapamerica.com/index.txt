Overview

Based in Nashville, Tennessee, Gen Cap America (GCA) is a partner with management teams who wish to invest in their companies by becoming part of a buyout group. Recapitalizations are also structured for business owners who desire immediate liquidity yet wish to participate in the growth of their business by retaining operating control.

Focusing on the middle market and lower middle market, GCA looks for investment candidates with revenues between $10 and $200M. Most are privately held businesses or subsidiaries or divisions of larger corporations. Capital investments from our fund are made in established businesses with demonstrated operating histories and quality management teams.
