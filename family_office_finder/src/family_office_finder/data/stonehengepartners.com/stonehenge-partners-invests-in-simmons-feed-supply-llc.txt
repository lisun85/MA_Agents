Stonehenge Partners Invests in Simmons Feed & Supply, LLC

March 2018: Stonehenge Partners invested in Simmons Feed & Supply, LLC (“Simmons” or the “Company”). Headquartered in Salem, Ohio, Simmons is a processor of organic soybeans for livestock feeds, and the Company produces organic lecithin for food and consumer product end markets. Simmons was founded in 1982 and is a third generation family-owned business.
