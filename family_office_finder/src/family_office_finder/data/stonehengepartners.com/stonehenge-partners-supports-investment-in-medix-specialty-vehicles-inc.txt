Stonehenge Partners Supports Investment in Medix Specialty Vehicles, Inc.

November 2017: Stonehenge Partners supported an investment in Medix Specialty Vehicles (“Medix” or the “Company”). Located in Elkhart, Indiana, Medix has been designing and manufacturing ambulances since 2001.

The Company manufactures Type I, Type II, and Type III ambulances for private ambulance operators, municipalities, hospitals, and health systems located across the United States and internationally. Medix produces one of the highest quality, best valued ambulances in the industry.
