Reva Dental Implant Centers ("Reva Dental") is a fixed arch dental implant company. Tacoma Holdings partnered with Dr. Jordan Swarbrick and Scott Felsted as co-founders of Reva Dental to facilitate a de novo growth strategy. Reva Dental partners with leading oral surgeons and prosthodontists to provide superior clinical outcomes and a consistently exceptional patient experience.
