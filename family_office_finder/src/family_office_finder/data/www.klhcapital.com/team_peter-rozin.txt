Peter Rozin
Associate

Peter is responsible for identifying investment opportunities, including initial financial, operating, and competitive analysis of businesses. Prior to joining KLH, Peter served as an Investment Banking Associate at EdgePoint, where he was responsible for financial modeling, material creation and advising clients on merger & acquisition transactions. Peter graduated from Emory University’s Goizueta Business School with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance.
