Austin Ray
Associate

Austin is responsible for executing transactions, due diligence and financial analysis, and portfolio management. Prior to joining KLH, Austin was an Investment Banking Associate at Baird in their Industrial Distribution Group. Earlier in his career, Austin served as a Senior Tax Consultant at Deloitte in the Business Tax Services practice. Austin is a registered CPA and graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and a Master of Accounting with a concentration in Taxation.
