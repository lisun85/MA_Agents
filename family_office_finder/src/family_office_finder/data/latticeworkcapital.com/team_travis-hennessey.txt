Travis Hennessey is a Vice President of LCM and is responsible for executing and managing investments across various healthcare industry sectors. He is currently a Board Observer of inReach Health and Beacon Behavioral Holdings. He formerly served as a Board Observer of Xpress Wellness.

Mr. Hennessey was previously an Investment Banking Analyst with Houlihan Lokey. While at Houlihan Lokey, Mr. Hennessey focused on executing mergers and acquisitions for clients across various healthcare verticals.

Mr. Hennessey graduated from Texas Christian University with a BBA in Accounting.
