Ruslan Gabidoulline is an Associate at LCM and is responsible for performing due diligence on, executing, and managing investments across various healthcare industry sectors.

Mr. Gabidoulline was previously an Investment Banking Analyst with William Blair. While at William Blair, Mr. Gabidoulline focused on executing mergers and acquisitions for clients across various industrial verticals, including Supply Chain Logistics and Aerospace and Defense.

Mr. Gabidoulline graduated Magna Cum Laude from University of California, Berkeley with a BS in Business Administration and a BA in Statistics.
