TODD MOWRY

Director, Operations

> WITH IOP SINCE 2019

Todd has over a decade of experience in the areas of profit and cash flow improvement, operations and process improvement, financial planning and analysis, profitable growth strategy, and mergers and acquisitions. At IOP, he works with the management teams of IOP’s portfolio companies, the firm’s Operating Principals, and other members of the Operations team to implement key operational and financial initiatives.

Prior to joining IOP, Todd served as a management consultant with The Keystone Group advising middle-market manufacturing and distribution companies. Previously he worked at Monsanto Company (acquired by Bayer) as a financial analyst and accountant.

Todd holds a BSBA in finance and accounting from Olin Business School at Washington University in St. Louis and an MBA with high honors from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. He is also a Certified Management Accountant (CMA).
