Ruchi is a Principal on the Investment Team. She has worked in the Education industry for over a decade. Prior to joining Renovus, Ruchi was an investment banker at BMO Capital Markets for six years, focusing on the Learning and Talent sector. She spent the early part of her career working in the Education non-profit industry and remains passionate about driving impact through education.
