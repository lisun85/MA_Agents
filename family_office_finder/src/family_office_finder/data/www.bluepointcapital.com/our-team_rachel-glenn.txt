Rachel rejoined the group in 2023. Most recently, she was a Senior Associate in the Capital Solutions group at Barings, preceded by her role as an Investment Banking Analyst at Deutsche Bank in New York City, focused on real estate, gaming, lodging and leisure.

Rachel graduated from Princeton University with a B.A. in Economics and a minor in Finance.

Outside of the office, Rachel enjoys staying active by running, playing soccer and lifting. She also loves spending time at Lake Norman, going to concerts and cheering on Philadelphia sports teams (Go Birds!).
