Our Team
Sai Parepally
Vice President

Sai Parepally is a Vice President and joined VSS in 2022. He is responsible for sourcing, evaluating, and structuring investment opportunities as well as working closely with portfolio companies.

Prior to joining VSS, Sai was an associate at Consonance Capital Partners, where he worked on a number of portfolio companies and transactions. He previously also worked in Investment Banking at Lazard Frères & Co, where he was involved in a number of marquee transactions, including the sale of Express Scripts to Cigna.

Sai holds a BS in Economics from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.
