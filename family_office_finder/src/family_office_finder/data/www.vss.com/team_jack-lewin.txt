Our Team
Jack Lewin
Associate

Jack Lewin is an Associate and joined VSS in 2024. He is responsible for evaluating, executing, and monitoring investments. Prior to joining VSS, Jack was an investment banking associate in the Consumer & Retail Group at Barclays, where he focused on M&A and capital markets transactions. He began his career as an investment banking analyst in the M&A group at Nomura.

Jack holds a BA in Economics and BS in Human and Organizational Development from Vanderbilt University.
