Investment Criteria
We look for experienced management teams seeking a capital partner with the same drive, ethics and desire to build a better and stronger company. These partnerships with management, Red River’s depth of experience, and our proven ability to unlock the full potential of companies allows success stories to be created.

 

TARGET CRITERIA:
Asset-light or low working capital
Founder-owned, entrepreneurial, growth oriented businesses
Key managers and/or founder(s) desiring to retain an equity stake and remain with the business in a transitional or long-term role
TRANSACTION TYPES:
Majority re-capitalization
Management / partnership buyout
COMPANY SIZE:
Revenue from $10m to $100m
EBITDA up to $15m
INDUSTRY FOCUS:
Business services
Inspection, repair, maintenance
Testing / measurement
Energy
Upstream
Downstream
Midstream
Oilfield Services
Light manufacturing and assembly
Short-run, quick turn-around, high volume
Technology
Technology-enabled services
Software as a service
