A&M Capital Strategic Investments (“AMCSI”), is AMC’s dedicated middle-market investment strategy focused on partnering with premier private equity firms in their core areas of expertise. AMCSI leverages the experience of its deal making professionals and unique affiliation with A&M Consulting’s market-leading private equity services business to generate robust deal flow from multiple proprietary origination channels and to facilitate value-added partnership. AMCSI’s professionals have significant experience in partnering with a broad set of leading private equity funds on a highly diverse set of investments.

TARGET SITUATIONS

We seek to invest in:

 

Active, co-sponsor, or syndicated partnerships with leading private equity firms on middle market leverage buyouts

“Equity Rollover” investments in A&M Capital portfolio companies at time of sale to third party investment funds

Recapitalizations of high-performing portfolio companies of private equity partners


“Mid-life” investments into portfolio companies of private equity funds to help fund M&A, facility build-outs, or other strategic activities


Structured equity solutions for middle market buyout transactions led by leading private equity firms

INVESTMENT APPROACH

We seek to:

 

Partner with premier private equity firms in their core areas of expertise

Focus on situations in which A&M Consulting can be a valuable difference maker in due diligence and/or post-acquisition value creation

Provide quick and reliable feedback to our private equity partners and operate with transparency and professionalism

Employ a diverse set of approaches and capabilities to formulate the most suitable partnership for a given investment situation

Create long-lasting relationships, leading to repeat transactions with private equity deal teams
